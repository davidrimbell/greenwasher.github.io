Lenovo Maintains Strong Rating in 2024 Hang Seng Corporate Sustainability Index – Receives Highest Environmental Score in the IT Industry

Lenovo has maintained its AA score in the Hang Seng Corporate Sustainability Index (HSSUS), showcasing its strong environmental achievements in the IT industry. The index, which evaluates companies on seven subjects under Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) categories, recognizes Lenovo s leadership in climate change and supplier engagement. The assessment process, conducted by the independent Hong Kong Quality Assurance Agency (HKQAA), is reviewed annually to reflect the evolving ESG landscape. Lenovo s commitment to sustainability is evident in its efforts to reduce emissions and increase participation in the circular economy. The company has reused or recycled over 94,000 metric tons of products through PELM programs since 2017, equivalent to the weight of 87 million ThinkPads. Lenovo has also invested more than US$50M in communities globally since 2022, focusing on Smarter AI for All and responsible AI development. Lenovo s ESG performance is detailed on Lenovo StoryHub and in the FY 2023/24 ESG Report. The company, a US$57 billion global technology powerhouse, is listed on the Hong Kong stock exchange and continues to innovate for a more equitable and smarter future. In summary, Lenovo s strong performance in the HSSUS reflects its dedication to sustainability, responsible AI development, and community investment. The company s efforts to reduce emissions, increase recycling, and support customers goals demonstrate its commitment to driving a more sustainable future for all.
