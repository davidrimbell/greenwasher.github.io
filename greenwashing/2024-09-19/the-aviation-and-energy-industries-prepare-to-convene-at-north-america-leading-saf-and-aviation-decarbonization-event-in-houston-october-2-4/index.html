The Aviation and Energy Industries Prepare to Convene at North America Leading SAF and Aviation Decarbonization Event in Houston , October 2 - 4

The Sustainable Aviation Futures North America Congress, scheduled for October 2-4, 2024, in Houston, will convene over 600 senior leaders from the aviation decarbonization sector. The event aims to discuss the necessary technology, policy, infrastructure, and collaboration to achieve aviation net-zero and scale Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) production by 2050. The congress will feature high-level strategic panel discussions, technical sessions, and interactive masterclasses, along with networking opportunities for forming critical partnerships. A dedicated Networking and Exhibition Zone, 1-2-1 meeting zone, and a new networking app will facilitate connections across the aviation and energy value chain. Key topics include the U.S. SAF Grand Challenge, SAF policy and incentives, global aviation decarbonization landscape, financing and investment, feedstock development, lifecycle analysis, corporate sustainability, green airports, climate resilience, and non-CO2 emissions. Industry sponsors supporting the congress include Shell Aviation, Montana Renewables, ExxonMobil, Johnson Matthey, and many others. Houston, known as the Energy Capital of the World, is an ideal location for this event due to its strong corporate presence, innovative research centers, and commitment to sustainability. For more information, contact Jamie Bentley, Marketing Director of Sustainable Aviation Futures. About Sustainable Aviation Futures: A high-level content community with a mission to scale global SAF production and establish pathways to aviation decarbonization through congresses, exclusive content, and in-depth training courses. The 2025 global portfolio of events includes Abu Dhabi, São Paulo, Amsterdam, Houston, and Singapore.
