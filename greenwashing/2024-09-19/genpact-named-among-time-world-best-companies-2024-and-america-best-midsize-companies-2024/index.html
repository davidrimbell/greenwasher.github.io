Genpact Named Among TIME World Best Companies 2024 and America Best Midsize Companies 2024

Genpact, a global professional services and solutions firm, has been recognized on TIME s World s Best Companies 2024 and America s Best Midsize Companies 2024 lists. This acknowledgment highlights Genpact s commitment to fostering a dynamic and inclusive employee culture, as well as its contributions to a more resilient and sustainable world. The TIME rankings, developed in collaboration with Statista, evaluate companies based on three key dimensions: employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability transparency. Independent surveys conducted with employees and industry experts from over 50 countries assess factors such as employee recommendations, financial performance, and environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices. Balkrishan BK Kalra, President and CEO of Genpact, emphasized the company s focus on leveraging its people-first culture and commitment to driving data, technology, and AI-led innovation to create a better future for clients, shareholders, talent, and communities. In addition to the TIME recognitions, Genpact has received other accolades, reinforcing its reputation as a preferred partner. The company, with its 125,000+ employees across 30+ countries, is driven by curiosity, entrepreneurial agility, and a desire to create lasting value for clients. Genpact serves and transforms leading enterprises, including the Fortune Global 500, with its deep business and industry knowledge, digital operations services, and expertise in data, technology, and AI. For more information about Genpact, visit their website (genpact.com) and follow them on LinkedIn, X, YouTube, and Facebook.
