CARIBBEAN AIRLINES LAUNCHES ITS SUSTAINABILITY PROGRAMME

Trinidad and Tobago s largest airline, Caribbean Airlines, has announced it is to launch a programme to reduce CO2 emissions by 2% in the first three years of its existence. The latest announcement is being released by the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO).
