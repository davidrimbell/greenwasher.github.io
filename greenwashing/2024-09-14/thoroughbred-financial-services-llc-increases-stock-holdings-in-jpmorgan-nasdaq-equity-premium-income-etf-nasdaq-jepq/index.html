Thoroughbred Financial Services LLC Increases Stock Holdings in JPMorgan Nasdaq Equity Premium Income ETF ( NASDAQ : JEPQ )

Thoroughbred Financial Services LLC increased its holdings in JPMorgan Nasdaq Equity Premium Income ETF (NASDAQ:JEPQ) by 3.3% during the second quarter, owning 70,991 shares worth $3,940,000. Other large investors also adjusted their stakes in the ETF, with Beacon Capital Management LLC and SJS Investment Consulting Inc. purchasing new positions valued at $26,000 and $28,000, respectively. TD Capital Management LLC acquired a $40,000 stake in the fourth quarter, while Global Wealth Strategies & Associates increased its position by 36.4% in the first quarter, now owning 750 shares valued at $41,000. Thurston Springer Miller Herd & Titak Inc. also added a $56,000 stake in the second quarter. JPMorgan Nasdaq Equity Premium Income ETF has a market capitalization of $15.26 billion, a P/E ratio of 32.10, and a beta of -0.84. The stock opened at $53.75 on Friday, with a 50-day moving average of $53.32 and a two-hundred day moving average of $53.64. The ETF recently announced a monthly dividend of $0.5569, paid on September 5th, representing a $6.68 annualized dividend and a yield of 12.43%. The ETF invests in large-cap US companies from the Nasdaq-100 Index, using ESG factors and a proprietary data science-driven investment approach.
