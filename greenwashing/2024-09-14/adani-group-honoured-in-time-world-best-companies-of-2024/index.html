Adani Group honoured in TIME World Best Companies of 2024

Adani Group, a leading integrated business conglomerate, has been featured in TIME magazine s World s Best Companies of 2024 list. The list, created in collaboration with Statista, evaluated 11 companies from the Adani portfolio, showcasing their comprehensive performance. The recognized companies include Adani Enterprises Ltd, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd, Adani Green Energy Ltd, Adani Energy Solutions Ltd, Adani Total Gas Ltd, Ambuja Cements, Adani Power Ltd, and Adani Wilmar Ltd. The accolade highlights the Adani Group s commitment to employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability. The company s emerging businesses, such as solar and wind manufacturing, airports, and roads, have contributed to a significant increase in net profit and EBITDA. In the first quarter of the fiscal year, the Adani Group s net profit rose by 50.1% to Rs 10,279 crore, while EBITDA reached Rs 22,570 crore, marking a 32.9% year-on-year increase. This recognition further validates the Adani Group s hard work and continuous efforts to push boundaries and deliver excellence across its diverse businesses. The World s Best Companies 2024 list is based on a rigorous analysis across three key dimensions – employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability (ESG). The Adani Group, India s largest and fastest-growing portfolio of diversified businesses, has demonstrated its commitment to these dimensions through its impressive performance and growth.
