BlackRock ESG Capital Allocation Term Trust ( ECAT ) to Issue Monthly Dividend of $0 . 30 on September 30th

BlackRock ESG Capital Allocation Term Trust (NYSE:ECAT) announced a monthly dividend of 0.3012 per share, payable to stockholders of record on September 16th, with the distribution scheduled for September 30th. This equates to an annualized dividend of $3.61 and a dividend yield of 20.29%. The ex-dividend date is set for September 16th. ECAT stock opened at $17.81 on a recent Friday, with a fifty-day simple moving average of $17.33 and a 200-day simple moving average of $17.15. The stock has experienced a 12-month low of $14.02 and a 12-month high of $18.14. BlackRock ESG Capital Allocation Trust aims to provide total return and income through a combination of current income, current gains, and long-term capital appreciation. The Trust invests in a portfolio of equity and debt securities, typically including both types of securities.
