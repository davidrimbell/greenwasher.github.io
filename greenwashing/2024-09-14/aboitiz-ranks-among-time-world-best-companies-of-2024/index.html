Aboitiz Ranks Among TIME World Best Companies Of 2024

The Aboitiz Group, a Philippine conglomerate, has been recognized alongside global giants like Apple, Microsoft, and BMW in TIME and Statista s World s Best Companies of 2024 ranking. This acknowledgment highlights the Group s commitment to sustainability, employee well-being, and innovation-driven transformation. As the Philippines first techglomerate, the Aboitiz Group leverages technology, innovation, and a startup culture to revolutionize its operations and amplify its impact. The Group s transformation aligns with the global trend of companies balancing financial success with positive societal impact. The Aboitiz Group s initiatives, such as CarbonPH, demonstrate its forward-thinking approach, using technology to support environmental and social good. Sabin M. Aboitiz, President and CEO of Aboitiz Group, emphasizes the company s people-first approach and commitment to being a force for good. The Group s tech-driven evolution aims to future-proof its operations, create meaningful jobs, and support the country s digital economy. The Aboitiz Group, with over a century of business history, operates across various sectors, including power, banking, food, land, infrastructure, and technology. As a member of the United Nations Global Compact and other sustainability initiatives, the Group is dedicated to advancing business and communities while contributing to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs). In summary, the Aboitiz Group s recognition as a techglomerate reflects its dedication to sustainability, employee well-being, and innovation-driven transformation. The Group s tech-powered evolution positions it as a leader in the Philippines business transformation, with a focus on creating a positive impact on society and the environment.
