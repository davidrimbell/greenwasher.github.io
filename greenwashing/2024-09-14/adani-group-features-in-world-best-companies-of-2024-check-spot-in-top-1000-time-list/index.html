Adani group features in world best companies of 2024 . Check spot in top 1000 TIME list

The Adani Group has been acknowledged in TIME s World s Best Companies of 2024 list, securing the 736th position. This accolade highlights the group s commitment to enhancing employee satisfaction, driving revenue growth, and promoting sustainability. The assessment covered eight out of eleven Adani companies, reflecting their broad business operations in multiple sectors. This recognition not only emphasizes the group s dedication to its core values but also its significant impact and presence in the global business landscape.
