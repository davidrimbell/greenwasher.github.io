Companies Collaborate to Establish Traceability and Certification System for Ethically Sourced Leather

In today’s news, we have some interesting updates on the topic of greenwashing and sustainability. Let’s dive right in!

First up, we have SMX (Security Matters) PLC and Domaine des Massifs joining forces to establish a comprehensive system for traceability and certification of raw materials in the leather industry. This collaboration aims to meet the growing consumer demand for ethically sourced products and set new standards for transparency and sustainability in the industry. It’s great to see companies taking responsibility and working towards a more sustainable future.

Moving on, Malaysia, as a signatory to the Paris Agreement, has committed to achieving net zero emissions by 2050. However, there seems to be a lack of urgency among small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in adopting environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices. To address this, the Star Media Group organized an ESG roundtable discussion, highlighting the need for incentives, policies, and grants to promote ESG adoption. It’s crucial for all businesses, regardless of size, to prioritize sustainability for the benefit of our planet.

In the financial sector, the U.S. Securities & Exchange Commission (SEC) has found an investment adviser misrepresenting its integration of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) factors. This serves as a reminder of the importance of robust education, monitoring compliance, and conducting quality checks. The adviser has taken steps to improve its practices, such as requiring analysts to consider ESG factors and conducting regular training sessions. It’s essential for financial services providers to uphold transparency and integrity when it comes to ESG practices.

Shifting our focus to the banking sector, Joseph Amo Adjei, the Manager of Risk Governance, ESG, and Sustainability at Stanbic Bank Ghana, emphasized the significance of ESG and Sustainability principles. He highlighted the impact of Sustainable Banking Principles in Ghana’s financial sector, ensuring a level playing field for all commercial banks. Adjei also mentioned the anticipated shift in capital deployment, where ESG evaluations will complement traditional creditworthiness assessments. It’s encouraging to see banking institutions aligning their practices with sustainability and responsibility principles.

In California, Governor Gavin Newsom is set to sign two climate disclosure bills into law, requiring large companies to disclose their emissions and report on climate-related financial risks. These bills align with international standards and will have a significant impact on businesses operating in the state. It’s a step forward in promoting transparency and accountability in the fight against climate change.

Caribbean Utilities Company, Ltd. has released its 2023 Sustainability Update Report, highlighting its progress on environmental, social, and governance initiatives. The company is committed to reducing CO2 emissions and increasing renewable energy sources. It’s inspiring to see companies setting ambitious targets and taking concrete actions towards a more sustainable future.

On the occasion of World Tourism Day, the focus this year is on “Tourism and green investment.” The event aims to highlight the importance of investing in education, sustainable infrastructure, and technology in the tourism sector. It’s crucial for the industry to integrate sustainability into tourism development projects and promote greener options for travelers.

Lastly, there have been claims that major media outlets are being funded by billionaire foundations and global elites to create panic about climate change. While it’s important to critically evaluate information, it’s equally crucial to recognize the urgency of addressing climate change and the need for collective action. Let’s focus on the facts and work towards a sustainable future.

That’s all for today’s news on greenwashing and sustainability. Stay informed, stay engaged, and let’s continue working towards a better world for future generations.
