Oldbury insurance specialist steps up net zero drive

Evolution Claims Management, a tech-driven firm based in Oldbury, has partnered with Sustainable Advantage, a carbon consultancy, to enhance its environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance. The collaboration aims to achieve a net zero balance between greenhouse gas emissions produced and removed from the atmosphere. Sustainable Advantage will handle data collation and verification, calculate the company s greenhouse gas emissions, and provide advice on carbon reduction strategies. The base year results were established by analyzing data from January to December 2023, with emission reduction goals set for 2030 and 2045. Managing director Russell Crewe emphasized the importance of insurers and service providers in building resilience to climate change impacts. Evolution Claims Management is committed to operating sustainably and responsibly, with a focus on reducing its carbon footprint. Client services director Ritchie Marshall highlighted that ESG is a necessity, not a choice, as both clients and investors demand it. The company has established company-wide objectives under the guidance of its ESG committee. Evolution Claims Management offers customer experience in claims management and building repairs for top insurance brands. The company operates on a fixed fee model, ensuring consistent charges regardless of repair value. In summary, Evolution Claims Management and Sustainable Advantage have joined forces to improve the company s ESG performance and reduce its carbon footprint. The partnership aims to achieve a net zero balance between greenhouse gas emissions produced and removed from the atmosphere, with goals set for 2030 and 2045. The company is committed to sustainable operations and has established company-wide ESG objectives.
