Agri Careers : movers and shakers

Angelo Gatto has been appointed CEO of Chanelle Pharma, leveraging his extensive 25-year experience in the pharmaceutical industry. He aims to utilize Chanelle Pharma s strong one-health positioning to foster the company s product pipeline. Jason Burbidge has been appointed Global Commercial Manager for the KEENAN division at Alltech. After a career in the agriculture sector, he is excited to return to KEENAN and contribute to shaping its future within the Alltech Group. Dr Elaine Mooney has joined the Safefood Advisory Board, where she will use her expertise in food education to promote healthy eating. She brings a wealth of knowledge in food science and nutrition to the board. Vincent Tennanty has been appointed as an MSAT specialist at Johnson & Johnson, where he will learn about human pharma manufacturing and advanced technologies like GenAI. Emma Walls has been appointed CEO of the National Dairy Council, where she will lead the organization through significant challenges and opportunities in the Irish dairy industry. Joey Dillon has been appointed as a sales representative for Bimeda, replacing retiring Morgan Lane. With a background in agriculture and experience with Moocall Ltd, Joey is eager to work with the experienced Bimeda sales team and support the customer base in his territory. These appointments highlight the industry s focus on expertise and experience in pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food science, as well as the importance of leadership and innovation in driving growth and success.
