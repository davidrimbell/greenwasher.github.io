NAB may lend to new gas projects under cap

Australia’s National Bank (NAB) has said it expects to provide debt funding for greenfield gas extraction in a state or federal area where the national interest is required, according to its chief executive, Andrew McEwan, in Sydney. They say the bank will only consider directly financing offshore gas projects.
