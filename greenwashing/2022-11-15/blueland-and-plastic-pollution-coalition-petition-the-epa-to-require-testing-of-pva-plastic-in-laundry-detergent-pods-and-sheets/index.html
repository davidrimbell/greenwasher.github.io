Blueland and Plastic Pollution Coalition Petition the EPA to Require Testing of PVA Plastic in Laundry Detergent Pods and Sheets

A petition to regulate the use of plastic film in laundry detergent pods and sheets has been launched by a coalition of environmental campaigners, including Blueland and Plastic Pollution Coalition, and Lonely Whale. Campaigners are calling for the regulation to be regulated.
