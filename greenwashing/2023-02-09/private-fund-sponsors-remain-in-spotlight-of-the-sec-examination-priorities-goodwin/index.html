Private Fund Sponsors Remain in Spotlight of the SEC Examination Priorities | Goodwin

The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) has issued its annual examination priorities for private fund sponsors in the coming financial year. These areas include compliance with the Marketing Rule and disclosure of conflicts of interest, cybersecurity and electronic communications.
