REP . JIM BANKS : America Can Only Move Forward Once We Defeat The Left Radicalism

House Republicans are intensifying their efforts to counter woke ideology, with a key piece of legislation led by Jim Banks aiming to combat the ESG (environmental, social, and governance) agenda. This agenda, according to Banks, is a means for the left to impose extreme views on gender, race, and climate on the American public. The Providing Complete Information to Retirement Investors Act, which Banks is championing, seeks to ensure retirees are aware of the risks associated with investing in funds that promote wokeism. Banks, who has experienced firsthand the repercussions of wokeism, including being blacklisted by Fortune 500 companies and having his government account suspended on Twitter, is determined to fight against this ideology. He founded the House Anti-Woke Caucus, the first of its kind in Congress, and has proposed bills to defund Biden s executive order on racial equity, require parental consultation in public schools on gender transition, and give minors harmed by transgender surgery the right to sue. Banks argues that wokeism is weakening America s national security, as it diverts attention from defending the nation. He also highlights the decline in patriotism and national pride, with the percentage of Americans who consider patriotism very important falling from 70% in 1998 to just 38% today. In addition to his work in Congress, Banks supports state-level actions against wokeism, such as Indiana s bill preventing state pension funds from investing in ESG funds. He emphasizes the need for Congress and state legislatures to act before wokeism destroys the values that make America great. Overall, Banks and other Republican lawmakers are dedicated to standing up to far-left ideologues and combating the harmful effects of wokeism on American society. They believe that opposing DEI (diversity, equity, and inclusion), divesting from ESG funds, and banning transgender surgeries for children are necessary steps to protect the nation s values and unity.
