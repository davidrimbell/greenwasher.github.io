Meritage Homes Third Quarter 2024 Earnings Conference Call and Webcast Scheduled for October 30 , 2024

Meritage Homes Corporation, a leading public homebuilder in the U.S., will release its third-quarter 2024 results on October 29, 2024. The company plans to discuss these results during a conference call on October 30, 2024, accessible via their Investor Relations page or by dialing a toll-free number. Meritage Homes, ranked as the fifth-largest homebuilder in the U.S. based on 2023 closings, specializes in energy-efficient and affordable entry-level and first move-up homes. The company operates in multiple states including Arizona, California, and Florida, among others. With a history spanning 38 years, Meritage Homes has delivered over 185,000 homes and is known for its distinctive style, quality construction, and award-winning customer experience. The company has received numerous accolades for its energy-efficient homebuilding, including the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency’s ENERGY STAR® Partner of the Year for Sustained Excellence Award, the Market Leader Award from the ENERGY STAR® Residential New Construction Program, and the EPA s Indoor airPLUS Leader Award. For more information, interested parties can visit Meritage Homes website or contact their VP of Investor Relations and ESG, Emily Tadano, via email or phone.
