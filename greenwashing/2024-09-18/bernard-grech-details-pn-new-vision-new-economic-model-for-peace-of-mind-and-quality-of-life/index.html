Bernard Grech details PN new vision : new economic model for peace of mind and quality of life

Nationalist Party leader Bernard Grech outlined his party s vision for Malta s future, emphasizing a new economic model that prioritizes peace of mind and quality of life for all citizens. Addressing a PN independence event, Grech highlighted the importance of education in equipping youths with necessary skills for the present and future. He stressed the need for the economic model to proactively anticipate future needs rather than reactively adjusting. Grech emphasized that the party s economic model aims to go beyond mere economic growth, focusing on improving citizens quality of life and ensuring that Maltese people can work, enjoy their loved ones, and be happy. He also highlighted the importance of infrastructure in supporting the envisioned economic model and praised past Nationalist governments for their contributions to Malta s infrastructure. Grech pledged that if elected, the Nationalist Party would invest in various economic sectors, including craftsmanship and manual labor, and commit to ESG principles. He also emphasized the party s commitment to meritocracy and pre-emptive planning, dismissing appointed positions without experience or knowledge. In conclusion, Grech called for Malta to progress by making important decisions for the future of the country, Gozo, and the Maltese people. He promised that the Nationalist Party s vision would lead to job creation and strong salaries, and that the party would invest in economic sectors centered around digital transformation. Grech urged people to trust his party to lead the way, citing the accomplishments of his party s MEPs and the selection of David Casa to lead an anti-corruption legislation. Grech s speech was delivered at a time when other speakers criticized the current state of affairs, economic model, planning, and the government s lack of self-criticism. Overall, Grech s vision for Malta s future centers on a new economic model that prioritizes peace of mind, quality of life, and proactive planning.
