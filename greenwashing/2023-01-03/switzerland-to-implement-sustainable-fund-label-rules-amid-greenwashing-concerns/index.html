Switzerland to implement sustainable fund label rules amid greenwashing concerns

The Swiss government is to introduce new rules for asset managers which label their products as sustainable, green and environmentally friendly funds, in a bid to tackle the issue of environmental waste. Why does this mean? They will be required to disclose how they meet the objective.
