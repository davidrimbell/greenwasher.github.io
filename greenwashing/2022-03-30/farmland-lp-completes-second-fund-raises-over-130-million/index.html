Farmland LP Completes Second Fund , Raises Over $130 Million

US farming giant Farmland LP has announced the closing of its second fund, raising more than $130 million in equity capital from individual investors and clients of registered investment advisors. The company has said the proceeds will be used to continue to invest in organic farms in the US.
