Ex - Central Bank Chief Dismisses Concerns of Multinational Greenwashing

China's former central bank governor has said there is no need to be a ban on corporate companies buying cheap carbon credits from developing countries, amid growing concerns about the impact of climate change on the global economy and its impact on greenhouse gases.
