Asahi Photoproducts Achieves Carbon Neutral Certification for AWP - DEW CleanPrint Flexo Plates

Asahi Photo Products has become the latest company to achieve a carbon neutral certification for its water-washable flexographic plates in compliance with the Pollution Standards Authority (PAS) 2060, which requires renewable carbon reductions to reduce the current carbon footprint.
