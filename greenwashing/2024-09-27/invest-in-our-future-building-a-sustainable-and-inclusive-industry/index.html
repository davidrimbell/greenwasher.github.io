Invest in Our Future : Building a Sustainable and Inclusive Industry

The Future Hospitality Summit (FHS World 2024) at Madinat Jumeirah in Dubai will focus on the theme Invest in Our Future, emphasizing the importance of investment in sustainability and diversity within the hospitality industry. The conference will explore content tracks such as Investment & Development, Destination & Urban Development, People, Planet, Profit, Tech & Innovation, and Real Estate Hotelisation. Industry partners have been asked about their investments in the future, highlighting the need for innovation and responsibility. The conference aims to guide owners and developers towards sustainable designs and foster diversity within the workforce. Sustainable investment in design and construction is crucial for a prosperous and sustainable future. Project teams must advocate for innovative solutions that enhance operational efficiencies and asset value, such as green technologies and energy-efficient systems. Hilton s research indicates that 84% of UAE residents prioritize minimizing environmental impact while traveling, increasing the demand for sustainable properties. Fostering diversity, particularly in the traditionally male-dominated design and construction industry, is essential for driving innovation and enhancing industry capabilities. Hilton is committed to supporting young female professionals through mentorship, career development resources, and promoting gender diversity in leadership roles. The conference calls for dedication and collaboration to advocate for sustainability and diversity principles. Investments in these areas will shape the industry s trajectory, leading to a brighter, more sustainable, and equitable future. Anne Bleeker from 2 Consulting emphasizes the importance of embracing this challenge with resolve and optimism, knowing that today s investments will yield a better future for the hospitality industry.
