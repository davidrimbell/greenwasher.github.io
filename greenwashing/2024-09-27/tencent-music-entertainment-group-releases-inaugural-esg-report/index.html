Tencent Music Entertainment Group Releases Inaugural ESG Report

Tencent Music Entertainment Group (TME), a leading online music and audio entertainment platform in China, has released its inaugural Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) report. The report underscores the company s dedication to sustainable practices and long-term value creation, outlining initiatives and performance in areas such as responsible platform, employee care, and social responsibility. Executive Chairman Mr. Cussion Pang emphasized the report s significance in showcasing the company s efforts to develop and implement a sustainability strategy. TME aims to continuously shape a sustainable future through its ESG initiatives. The report can be accessed at https://ir.tencentmusic.com/ESG-Reports. TME operates popular music apps like QQ Music, Kugou Music, Kuwo Music, and WeSing, offering a comprehensive platform for music discovery, listening, singing, live streaming, and socializing. The press release includes a safe harbor statement, noting that the information provided is forward-looking and subject to risks and uncertainties. The company does not guarantee the accuracy of the statements and encourages investors to review the company s filings with the SEC and HKEX for further information on risks and uncertainties.
