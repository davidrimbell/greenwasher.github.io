Finance leaders to convene at 6th MENA Investment Congress

The 6th edition of the MENA Investment Congress (MENA ICON), organized by the CFA Society Emirates, is scheduled to take place at the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) on September 30 and October 1, 2024. This prestigious event is anticipated to convene top finance, investment, and policy-making experts for two days of insightful discussions on critical issues affecting the global and regional financial landscape. MENA ICON 2024 aims to provide a platform for finance professionals to engage in meaningful dialogues on topics that significantly impact investment decisions. The event s agenda will address key challenges such as geopolitical trends, asset allocation in volatile markets, the role of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in finance, and the increasing importance of environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors. The congress will feature keynote presentations and expert panel discussions, offering actionable insights from renowned global and regional finance leaders. Additionally, knowledge enhancement workshops will be available for CFA charterholders and finance professionals, focusing on advanced valuation strategies, personal branding, and the impact of AI on investment decision-making. Set in the UAE s international financial center, MENA ICON 2024 will facilitate networking opportunities among senior executives, asset managers, institutional investors, and policymakers. The event will be managed by Quiet Loud, a leading event tech platform, ensuring a seamless and impactful experience for participants. Quiet Loud s expertise in cutting-edge event technologies and bespoke event solutions will create a dynamic and immersive environment that promotes learning, networking, and collaboration among finance professionals. The event will also feature two Google Adsense ads, which are not part of the summary and have been removed for clarity.
