Air Canada hosted One Young World Summit

Air Canada hosted the One Young World Summit in Montreal, focusing on diversity, inclusivity, and sustainable futures. Over 2,000 young leaders from 190 countries gathered to discuss Indigenous empowerment and collaborative efforts between the private sector and NGOs. The summit, which took place over three days, showcased Air Canada s commitment to inclusivity and diversity, aligning with One Young World s mission to create a sustainable future where all voices are heard. The event featured an inspiring panel on Indigenous empowerment, led by Air Canada s Head of Investor Relations and Corporate Sustainability, Valérie Durand. The panel highlighted the leadership and vision of three Indigenous youth committed to creating positive change in their communities. Air Canada s delegates, representing various fields such as AI, Community Impact, and Environmental Affairs, proudly flew the flag at the summit. The airline also provided an Indigenous Voices space, where participants could learn about Indigenous culture and history. Throughout the event, attendees interacted with Air Canada s team of delegates and kiosk representatives to learn about the airline s environmental sustainability initiatives. The Air Canada pledge wall allowed visitors to write down their commitment to change, and a quiz station offered prizes for those who tested their knowledge. The summit s diverse range of voices and perspectives created a rich environment for innovative solutions. Air Canada s role as the official airline of the event allowed participants to connect with the airline s mission and values, fostering a sense of unity and purpose among the attendees.
