GlobalCapital Global Derivatives Awards : the winners !

The GlobalCapital annual Awards dinner celebrated achievements in the global derivatives market, with BNP Paribas taking top honors in multiple categories. The French bank s client-centric approach, commitment to integrated services, and tailormade solutions were highlighted. BNP Paribas also won the Thought Leader in ESG award, reflecting its holistic approach to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) issues. Other notable winners included CME Group for Global Exchange of the Year, and Latham & Watkins for Global Law Firm of the Year. The awards recognized innovation, advocacy, growth, and exceptional client service across various categories such as Equity, FX, Structured Products, and Research & Strategy. Despite challenges in 2023, organizations demonstrated resilience by providing liquidity and innovative solutions. The selection process involved extensive research, over 100 pitch meetings, and a global editorial panel. The full list of winners is available for download. The event was made possible by the support of sponsors, and congratulations were extended to all nominees and winners. The awards aimed to recognize the best in the industry, promoting excellence and innovation in the global derivatives market.
