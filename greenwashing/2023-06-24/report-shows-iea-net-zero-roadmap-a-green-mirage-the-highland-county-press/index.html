Report shows IEA Net Zero Roadmap a green mirage | The Highland County Press

The threat of a cessation of investment in new oil and gas fields has been revealed by the Realclear Foundation. () The Real Clear Foundation says it is being asked to investigate why it doesn t be able to explain the dangers of an immediate end to new investments in renewable energy projects in the UK and the world, and how it will affect investors and investment managers. But what is it likely to have to do to stop their efforts to tackle climate change and increase global demand for energy infrastructure? The realclear foundation has called for an analysis of its latest report on emissions targets and its impact on Western companies. What is the reason?
