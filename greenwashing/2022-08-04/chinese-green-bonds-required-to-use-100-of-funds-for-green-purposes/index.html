Chinese green bonds required to use 100 % of funds for green purposes

China’s new Green Bond Pprinciples have been published to mark the end of a long-running dispute over green bonds issued by international issuers.The new Prinsips aims to ensure that 100% of the funds raised by the four major types of green bond in China be used for green projects.
