H2B2 and 2G Energy Receive 2024 CHP Project of the Year Award for SoHyCal Renewable Hydrogen Impact

H2B2 USA LLC and 2G Energy, Inc. have been awarded the 2024 CHP Project of the Year by the Combined Heat and Power Alliance for their collaborative SoHyCal project. The project, which focuses on producing renewable hydrogen through electrolysis powered by renewable energy, is a significant advancement in sustainable energy solutions in California. The project, managed by H2B2, integrates cutting-edge biogas technology from 2G Energy to ensure carbon-neutrality and align with California s climate goals. CEO of H2B2, Pedro Pajares, highlighted the importance of collaboration in achieving the project s success, emphasizing that the SoHyCal project represents progress towards a sustainable future. Managing Director of 2G Energy, Darren Jamison, also expressed pride in their partnership and commitment to advancing renewable hydrogen solutions. The award recognizes the pioneering effort of the SoHyCal project, showcasing the successful integration of H2B2 and 2G Energy s innovative technologies. The project not only meets current clean hydrogen demands but also sets a foundation for future expansions to support California s climate goals and inspire similar global initiatives. H2B2 USA LLC is a California-based company specializing in renewable hydrogen production and integrated solutions for the entire hydrogen value chain. 2G Energy, Inc. is a global leader in energy efficiency and power generation, with over 8,500 CHP power plants installed in more than 30 countries. For more information, visit www.h2b2.es and https://www.2g-energy.com/. SOURCE: 2G Energy News, published on September 23, 2024.
