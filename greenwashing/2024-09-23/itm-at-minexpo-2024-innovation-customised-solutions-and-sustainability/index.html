ITM At Minexpo 2024 : Innovation , Customised Solutions And Sustainability

ITM Group, a leader in undercarriage sensor technology, will be showcasing its latest advancements at MINExpo 2024, the world s largest mining event. Held from September 24-26 in Las Vegas, the Group will present its innovative TRUST ITM® tracking system, which monitors undercarriage status, reduces downtime, and enhances safety and productivity. The system uses wireless sensors and IoT technology to provide real-time data on key parameters through the TrackAdvice® platform, a proprietary software developed by ITM for optimal undercarriage management. ITM Group, founded in 1957, offers a comprehensive range of undercarriage parts and customized solutions for various industry segments, including mining, construction, paving, agriculture, forestry, marine, and more. The company operates through a global service network, dealers, and distributors, with production facilities in Italy, Germany, Spain, USA, Brazil, China, Australia, and a joint venture in India. ITM Group emphasizes environmental responsibility, working on solutions to reduce the environmental impact of its production processes and machines using its components. For more information, visit the company s website. About ITM Group: ITM Group designs, manufactures, and distributes a wide range of undercarriage parts and customized solutions for several industrial sectors. The company has over 1,600 professionals and engineers worldwide and operates through a global service network, dealers, and distributors.
