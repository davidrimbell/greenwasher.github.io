CSRWire - Navigating Scope 3 : Estée Lauder Path to Carbon Reduction

In the concluding episode of the ESG Talk Climate Week series, Nancy Mahon, Estée Lauder Companies chief sustainability officer, discusses the company s Scope 3 measurement and reporting strategies with host Alyssa Zucker. The conversation delves into the influence of consumer sustainability preferences and the complexities of spearheading sustainability initiatives across a varied range of brands. The episode also serves as an invitation to subscribe to the ESG Talk podcast, available on Apple, Spotify, and YouTube. The podcast is produced by Workiva, a global platform that specializes in financial reporting, ESG, audit, and risk. Workiva aims to empower transparent reporting for a better world by providing regulatory, financial, and ESG reporting solutions. Workiva s mission is to simplify complex reporting and disclosure challenges through a cloud-based platform that connects data and teams, ensuring consistency. The company s platform is designed to meet stakeholder demands for action, transparency, and disclosure of both financial and non-financial data. For more information about Workiva and its services, visit workiva.com.
