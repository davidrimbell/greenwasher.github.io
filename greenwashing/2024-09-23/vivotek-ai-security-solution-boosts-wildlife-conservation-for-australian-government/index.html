VIVOTEK AI Security Solution Boosts Wildlife Conservation for Australian Government

The project, a joint venture between Zoos Victoria and Parks Victoria, aimed to monitor seals on haul-out structures in Port Phillip Bay, Australia. Zoos Victoria, a renowned zoo-based conservation organization, and Parks Victoria, the state government authority for managing protected areas, collaborated to leverage VIVOTEK s advanced surveillance technology for wildlife conservation. VIVOTEK s Speed Dome cameras, connected to AI servers, were installed on the structures to detect entanglements, wounds, and monitor seal health, providing real-time data to the Marine Response Unit for prompt medical intervention. The project faced challenges due to the remote location and harsh marine environment. The cameras were powered by solar energy, with 4G LTE connectivity and a large lithium power pack to ensure continuous operation. The cameras needed to withstand extreme conditions and provide high-resolution, real-time video feeds over long distances. VIVOTEK s solution showcased its commitment to innovation and sustainability. The Speed Dome cameras, with features like 5MP resolution, 200M IR illuminators, and 30x optical zoom, were housed in IP66, IK10, and NEMA 4X-rated enclosures, ensuring durability in extreme weather conditions. The AI-powered system provided advanced monitoring capabilities, surpassing traditional surveillance norms. The project received positive feedback for its contribution to wildlife preservation and conservation efforts. The system s efficiency in monitoring seal health and detecting distress signals was praised by the Marine Response Unit and other stakeholders. This collaboration between Zoos Victoria, Parks Victoria, and VIVOTEK exemplified the potential for security technology to be applied differentially, focusing on animal protection and conservation. Overall, the project highlighted VIVOTEK s role as a leading provider of advanced security solutions, committed to making a positive environmental impact through meaningful government collaborations. The success of this project showcased VIVOTEK s innovative AI technology and its dedication to leveraging cutting-edge technology for the betterment of wildlife conservation.
