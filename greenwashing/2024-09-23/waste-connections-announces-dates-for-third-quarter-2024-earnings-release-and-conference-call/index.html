WASTE CONNECTIONS ANNOUNCES DATES FOR THIRD QUARTER 2024 EARNINGS RELEASE AND CONFERENCE CALL

Waste Connections, Inc. (TSX/NYSE: WCN), a leading integrated solid waste services company, announced its financial results for the third quarter of 2024 will be released on October 23, 2024. The company will host an investor conference call on October 24th at 8:30 A.M. Eastern Time. The call will be live-streamed on their website under News & Events. Participants can preregister for the call and receive dial-in instructions and a personalized code for entry. A replay of the call will be available until October 31, 2024. Waste Connections serves approximately nine million residential, commercial, and industrial customers across 46 U.S. states and six Canadian provinces. The company also provides non-hazardous oilfield waste treatment, recovery, and disposal services in several basins across the U.S. and Canada, as well as intermodal services for cargo and solid waste containers in the Pacific Northwest. The company emphasizes its commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) efforts, focusing on reducing emissions, increasing resource recovery of recyclable commodities and clean energy fuels, reducing reliance on off-site disposal for landfill leachate, improving safety, and enhancing employee engagement. For more information on their progress towards targeted achievement, visit wasteconnections.com/sustainability. For further inquiries, contact Mary Anne Whitney at (832) 442-2253 or Joe Box at (832) 442-2153.
