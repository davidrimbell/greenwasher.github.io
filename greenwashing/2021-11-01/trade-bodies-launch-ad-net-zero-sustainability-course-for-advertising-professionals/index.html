Trade bodies launch Ad Net Zero sustainability course for advertising professionals

The Independent Advertising Agency (IPA) has launched a new climate change training course for advertising workers in the UK and Ireland, which aims to tackle the threat posed by the coronavirus pandemic. They will also be offered the chance to put their knowledge to the test.
