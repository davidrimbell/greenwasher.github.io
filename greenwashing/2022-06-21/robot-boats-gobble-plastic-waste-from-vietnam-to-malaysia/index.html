Robot boats gobble plastic waste from Vietnam to Malaysia

A robotic boat that gobbles up plastic bottles, wrappers and other rubbish that could spill into the Pacific Ocean has joined a flotilla of similar craft making waves from Malaysia to Indonesia that have been built by the Ocean Cleanup nonprofit.
