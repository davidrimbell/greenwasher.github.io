Change of Name | Company Announcement

On September 16, 2024, JLEN Environmental Assets Group Limited (JLEN) changed its name to Foresight Environmental Infrastructure Limited (FGEN) following a shareholder approval at the AGM held on September 13. The name change became effective on September 16, and the stock market ticker was updated to FGEN starting September 17 at 08:00 a.m. The company s website was also updated to https://www.fgen.com/. Despite the name change, shareholders shareholdings remain unaffected, and existing share certificates will continue to be valid. FGEN s investment policy focuses on a diversified portfolio of Environmental Infrastructure, which includes assets, projects, and businesses that utilize natural or waste resources, support environmentally friendly economic activities, or mitigate climate change effects. These investments typically feature long-term, predictable cash flows, stable regulatory frameworks, and well-established technologies. FGEN aims to provide investors with a sustainable, progressive dividend per share, paid quarterly, while preserving the capital value of the portfolio over the long term on a real basis. The target dividend for the year ending March 31, 2025, is 7.80 pence per share. As an Article 9 fund under the EU Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation, FGEN has a transparent and award-winning approach to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) factors. Further information about the company can be found on its website, which will be live from September 17, 2024. The company s Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) remains unchanged at 213800JWJN54TFBMBI68. For further information, contact Foresight Group, Winterflood Securities Limited, Apex Fund and Corporate Services (Guernsey), or any of the listed representatives.
