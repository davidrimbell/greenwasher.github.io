Carnival Corporation Named One of the World Best Companies of 2024 ; World largest cruise company honored on TIME annual listing of the top - performing companies worldwide

Carnival Corporation & plc, the world s largest cruise company, has been recognized as one of the World s Best Companies of 2024 by TIME. The company ranked in the top 100 overall and first among cruise companies in the comprehensive analysis based on employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainable performance in environmental protection, social responsibility, and corporate governance (ESG). This accolade builds on Carnival Corporation s previous honors for employment excellence, diversity, equity, and inclusion, and sustainability. The company s success is attributed to its over 160,000 team members who deliver extraordinary cruise vacations while upholding the integrity of every ocean sailed, place visited, and life touched. Carnival Corporation s commitment to sustainable cruising, strong shareholder value, and a positive environment for all team members is further highlighted by its recognition as one of the World s Best Employers by Forbes and a Best Place to Work for LGBTQ+ Equality for seven consecutive years by the Human Rights Campaign Foundation. The company s industry-leading sustainability initiatives can be found on its website. For more information, visit Carnival Corporation s website and media contacts.
