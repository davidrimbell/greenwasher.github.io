Transform Your Burger Game with PleatPak | RestaurantNews . com

GreenDustries PleatPak® has emerged as a revolutionary eco-friendly and cost-effective solution in the global burger packaging market, setting a new standard for sustainability and functionality. Invented by Akiva Buchberg, the President of GreenDustries, PleatPak® addresses the prevalent issue of greenwashing in the foodservice packaging industry by offering a truly sustainable product. With 60-70% of consumers willing to pay more for sustainable packaging, PleatPak® not only drives consumer interest and loyalty but also saves operators money due to its innovative design. PleatPak® is recognized as the best burger packaging alternative, with its unique spherical design and perfect fit minimizing waste in landfills and kitchens. The packaging has won numerous awards for its superior functionality and sustainability, including endorsement from the Dogwood Alliance, a leading environmental US forests protection NGO. The PleatPak® design has been praised by customers and restaurant operators alike for its ability to retain heat and juices, ensuring the integrity of the burger throughout the delivery and consumption process. A study conducted by GreenDustries showed that PleatPak® outperformed clamshells, foil laminated wraps, and quilt wraps in heat retention, a major concern for today s consumers. PleatPak® is sold in more than 70 countries and has been embraced by iconic venues and better burger chains worldwide. Its small footprint makes it easy to store and quick to teach to employees, providing a practical solution for back-of-house operations. In conclusion, PleatPak® by GreenDustries embodies the essence of innovation and sustainability, providing a cost-effective, heat-retaining, and user-friendly packaging solution. It not only meets consumers desires but also aligns with industry needs, bridging the gap between environmental consciousness and practicality in the world of fast food and eating on the go. With its unique design and superior functionality, PleatPak® is set to become the overwhelming wrap of choice
