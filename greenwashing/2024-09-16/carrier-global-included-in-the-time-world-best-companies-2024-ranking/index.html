Carrier Global : Included in the TIME World Best Companies 2024 Ranking

Carrier Global Corporation, a leading company in intelligent climate and energy solutions, has been recognized in the TIME World s Best Companies 2024 ranking. The ranking, conducted by TIME and Statista, evaluates companies based on employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability (ESG). The assessment involved surveys from over 170,000 participants across 50 countries, examining factors such as company image, working conditions, and equality. Carrier s inclusion in the ranking highlights its success in these three dimensions. The company has demonstrated positive revenue growth from 2021 to 2023, with a minimum revenue of US $100 million in 2023. Additionally, Carrier s commitment to sustainability is evident through its ESG data and targeted research. Carrier Global Corporation is dedicated to creating solutions that benefit both people and the planet for future generations. The company has a diverse workforce that prioritizes customer satisfaction. For more information, interested parties can visit Carrier s corporate website or follow them on Twitter. Statista, a leading data and business intelligence portal, publishes hundreds of worldwide industry rankings and company listings. The research and analysis service provided by Statista is based on the success of statista.com, which offers statistics, business-relevant data, and various market and consumer studies and surveys. In summary, Carrier Global Corporation s inclusion in the TIME World s Best Companies 2024 ranking reflects its success in employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability. The company s commitment to creating solutions for people and the planet, along with its diverse workforce, sets it apart in the industry. Statista s research and analysis service, based on the success of its data and business intelligence portal, supports the recognition of top-performing companies globally.
