Automechanika Shanghai 2024

Automechanika Shanghai 2024, set for December 2-5, will focus on sustainable innovations in the automotive industry, featuring over 6,500 exhibitors and 14 country and region pavilions across 350,000 sqm at the National Exhibition and Convention Center. The event, marking its 20th anniversary, aims to connect Chinese companies with global markets and provide insights into China s rapidly developing new energy vehicle (NEV) market. Fiona Chiew, general manager of Messe Frankfurt (HK), expresses confidence in the event s success, citing the growing interest from the industry and the opportunity for marketing, trade, and education. The show will highlight advancements in NEVs, connectivity, autonomous driving, and traditional segments, while also engaging with related sectors like transportation, retail, e-commerce, and advanced technology. The International Automotive Industry Conference 2024, presented by Automechanika Shanghai, will be a key highlight, bringing together industry leaders to discuss global trends, supply chain transformation, and cross-border cooperation. The Innovation4Mobility Showcase Areas will also feature seminars on automotive AI, digital transformation, and sustainable development. To enhance the visitor experience, thematic routes will be introduced, and interactive elements like the stepathlon for tree planting and Quest for a Racing Master competition will be held. Exhibitors are encouraged to start promoting early, with additional advertising and sponsorship opportunities available. Automechanika Shanghai serves as a vital platform for networking, lead generation, and product launches, with thousands of delegates expected to visit over 6,500 exhibitors. The event will foster collaboration and explore new market prospects, making it essential for building relationships in the automotive industry. In addition to the main show, various fringe events will cover different sectors and areas along the supply chain, including the International Summit of Automotive Industry Talent Development, International Summit of Connected-Vehicle Policies and Regulations, and Automotive Aftermarket Summit.
