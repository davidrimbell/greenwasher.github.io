HCLTech ranks # 1 India - headquartered company in TIME Magazine World Best Companies 2024

HCLTech, a prominent global technology company headquartered in India, has been recognized as the highest-ranked Indian company in TIME Magazine s World s Best Companies 2024. This accolade reflects HCLTech s exceptional performance in areas such as employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and environmental, social, and governance (ESG) metrics. The company has secured the top position among all India-headquartered companies and is also ranked within the top 10 globally in the Professional Services category. This recognition is the second consecutive year for HCLTech in these prestigious rankings, highlighting its commitment to excellence and innovation. Rahul Singh, HCLTech s COO of Corporate Functions, emphasized the company s dedication to its purpose of Supercharging Progress and Find Your Spark. HCLTech aims to maximize employee potential, advance community and social responsibility initiatives, and achieve sustainability goals. The World s Best Companies 2024 ranking is based on three primary dimensions: Employee Satisfaction, Revenue Growth, and Sustainability (ESG). The evaluation involved surveys conducted in over 50 countries, with data collected from approximately 170,000 participants. Companies generating revenue of at least US $100 million in 2023 and demonstrating positive revenue growth from 2021 to 2023 were considered. ESG data was evaluated based on standardized KPIs from Statista s ESG database and targeted data research. HCLTech s latest accolade further strengthens its impressive list of awards, including recognition as a Forbes Best Employer for New Graduates, a Forbes Best Management Consulting Firm, and a Global Top Employer by the Top Employers Institute. These honors collectively underscore HCLTech s unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and employee satisfaction.
