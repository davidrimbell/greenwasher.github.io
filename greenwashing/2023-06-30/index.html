However, the question of funding for sustainability efforts remains largely unaddressed.

Indie Semiconductor has published its first Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) report, showcasing its commitment to environmental sustainability, social responsibility, and corporate governance. By emphasizing its dedication to these areas, the company aims to avoid misrepresentation and deceit in conducting business globally.

Glencore CEO Gary Nagle has criticized European investors for prioritizing ESG factors over financial returns, accusing them of dishonesty. He defends U.S. investors as more pragmatic and focused on yield. Nagle’s remarks come as some oil and gas majors, such as Shell and BP, have higher valuations in the United States despite softening their climate commitments. Institutional Shareholder Services (ISS) is planning to change its methodology for its Environmental & Social Disclosure QualityScore (ISS QualityScore) to improve its measures of corporate disclosure practices. This move aims to combat false advertising and deceit by encouraging companies to review and verify their data to ensure compliance with the updated methodology. The ISS QualityScore helps institutional investors compare environmental and social risks through company disclosures, promoting transparency and discouraging greenwashing.

Rogers Corporation has issued an update to its 2021 Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Report, highlighting its commitment to corporate responsibility and sustainability. By extending reporting quality and including third-party verification of greenhouse gas inventories, the company aims to avoid misrepresentation and dishonesty. Rogers Corporation’s dedication to sustainability is evident in its innovative solutions for various industries, including electric vehicles, renewable energy, and wireless infrastructure.

ESG.org has launched a new resource called WhatisESG to support ESG practitioners by providing research materials, presentation materials, and educational content. This initiative aims to combat eco-washing and sustainability washing by compiling reputable sources and categorizing them in an easy-to-use format. The organization hopes that ESG practitioners will find WhatisESG to be a valuable tool in promoting genuine sustainability efforts.
