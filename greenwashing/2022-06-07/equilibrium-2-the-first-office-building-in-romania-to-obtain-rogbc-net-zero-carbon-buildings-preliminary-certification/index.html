Equilibrium 2 , the first office building in Romania to obtain RoGBC - Net Zero Carbon Buildings preliminary certification

A second phase of a project to reduce CO2 emissions in Romania has become the first building in the country to be given the new certification for sustainable office building development in Europe, according to the Romanian Green Building Council (RoGBC) and the government.
