Builder Merchants News - BCF releases guide against greenwashing in decorative paints marketing

A campaign has been launched to tackle the rising number of green claims made online about decorative paints, which could be misleading consumers with ostensibly eco-friendly terms, such as ‘non-toxic’, ‘vegan’ and ‘Organic’.
