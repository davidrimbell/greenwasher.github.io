Princeton to divest from some sectors of the fossil fuel industry

The University of Princeton has announced plans to disassociate its thermal coal and tar sands sectors from the fossil fuel industry, as part of efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and tackle climate disinformation, according to the University Board of Trustees.
