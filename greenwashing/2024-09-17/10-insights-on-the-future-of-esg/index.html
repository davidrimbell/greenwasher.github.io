10 Insights on the Future of ESG

The Aspen Institute Business & Society Program s Aspen ESG Summit in the Summer of 2024 convened 200 influential leaders in an invitation-only event focused on business transformation and sustainable innovation. The summit aimed to foster dialogue among business leaders, long-term investors, corporate governance experts, and regulators to address the increasing demands on business practices. The discussions centered on developing new leadership capacities and operational strategies at a faster pace and scale, as well as reimagining engagement with employees, customers, suppliers, and broader stakeholders. The event produced 10 key insights on the future of ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance), derived from conversations and onsite polling. These insights highlighted the urgent need for sustainable innovation and the importance of collaborative efforts among various stakeholders to drive change. The summit also generated big ideas for 2025, aiming to guide the ongoing evolution of ESG practices in the business world. Overall, the Aspen ESG Summit served as a platform for leaders to exchange ideas, explore emerging issues, and shape the future of sustainable business practices. The event emphasized the importance of collaboration, innovation, and adaptability in addressing the challenges and opportunities presented by the rapidly changing business landscape.
