iShares ESG Aware USD Corporate Bond ETF ( NASDAQ : SUSC ) Hits New 1 - Year High at $23 . 93

iShares ESG Aware USD Corporate Bond ETF (NASDAQ:SUSC) reached a new 52-week high on Tuesday, trading at $23.93 with a volume of 15,402 shares. The iShares ESG Aware USD Corporate Bond ETF invests in USD-denominated, investment-grade corporate debt with positive ESG characteristics, aiming to replicate the risk and return profile of the Bloomberg US Corporate Index.
