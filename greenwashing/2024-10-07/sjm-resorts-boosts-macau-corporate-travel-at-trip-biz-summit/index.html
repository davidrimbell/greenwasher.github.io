SJM Resorts boosts Macau corporate travel at Trip . Biz summit

The main conclusion from the article is that SJM Resorts, a leading integrated tourism and leisure enterprise, has partnered with Trip.Biz at the Seoul summit to promote Macau as a top destination for corporate travelers. This collaboration aims to showcase Macau s unique offerings and align with the Macau SAR Government s push for technology-driven tourism. SJM Resorts, with over 60 years of experience in Macau, operates the Lisboa brand of hotels and resorts, including the Grand Lisboa Palace Resort Macau, the only integrated resort in the world with two fashion-branded hotels under one roof. The company actively promotes Macau s culture and attractions, supporting the Macau government s vision of establishing Macau as a World Centre of Tourism and Leisure. The partnership with Trip.Biz provides corporate travelers with seamless and consistent travel solutions, showcasing SJM s luxurious offerings from five-star accommodations, award-winning dining, to versatile banqueting and meeting venues. Trip.Biz, a corporate travel management brand under Trip.com Group, offers all-in-one digital business travel solutions and the latest Trip.Biz Product Matrix to a global audience. The Trip.Biz Product Matrix features six core offerings: Trip.Biz OBT & App, Trip.Biz Reporting, Trip.Biz Payment, Trip.Biz Desk, Trip.Biz ESG, and Trip.Biz Care. These innovations address the evolving needs of modern global business travel on all fronts. Daisy Ho, Managing Director of SJM, emphasizes the company s commitment to supporting Macau s development as a World Centre of Tourism and Leisure by creating diversified Tourism+ products that showcase Macau s unique charm and cultural heritage. The collaboration between SJM and Trip.Biz aims to drive innovation and enrich the visitor experience through advanced technologies, as seen in attractions like AI Wonderland and Martial Art
