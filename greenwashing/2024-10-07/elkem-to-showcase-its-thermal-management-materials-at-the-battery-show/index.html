ELKEM TO SHOWCASE ITS THERMAL MANAGEMENT MATERIALS AT THE BATTERY SHOW

Elkem, a renowned silicone solutions provider, will participate in The Battery Show in Detroit from October 7-10, showcasing its innovative battery assembly solutions. The company s booth, #4605, will feature its Thermal Management Materials, designed to enhance battery performance and safety by mitigating thermal runaway, dissipating heat, and improving fire resistance. Elkem s solutions have protected over two million EV battery packs with 60 billion miles driven. John Albaugh, associate market manager, highlights Elkem s ability to run thermal simulations to demonstrate the benefits of their products. Attendees can expect hands-on displays and literature on Elkem s solutions. Albaugh emphasizes the company s commitment to meeting customer needs and its agility in innovating into emerging markets. Elkem Silicones, with over 70 years of experience in automotive applications, is dedicated to creating a sustainable future through innovation. The company s EV Applications Center aims to reduce application development costs, expedite validation, and accelerate time to market. Elkem s thermal insulating materials and their use in EV batteries are protected by patents US10,501,597 B2 & US11,261,309 B2 and patent applications US2022081529 & US2023203265. The company, listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange (ELK), has achieved an operating income of NOK 35.5 billion in 2023 and received CDP ratings of A on Forests, and A- on Climate Change and Water Security.
