Sai Life Sciences releases Sustainability Report 2024

Sai Life Sciences, a rapidly expanding Indian Contract Research, Development, and Manufacturing Organization (CRDMO), has released its Sustainability Report for 2023-2024, emphasizing the integration of sustainability, performance, and growth. The report, adhering to the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) Universal Standards 2021 framework, highlights the company s commitment to climate action and sustainable development goals (SDGs). CEO Krishna Kanumuri announced that Sai Life Sciences has achieved significant progress in reducing greenhouse gas emissions, increasing renewable energy consumption to over 50%, and lowering specific water usage. The company has also exceeded its CSR goal of providing education and livelihoods to 8,000 individuals from underprivileged communities. Sai Life Sciences has made notable strides in advancing its sustainability agenda, including joining the Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) to combat climate change, becoming the first India-headquartered company to join the Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Initiative (PSCI) membership, and strengthening its culture of Health & Safety. The company has also received several prestigious awards for its excellence in Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) practices and energy management. With a workforce comprising 14.3% women, Sai Life Sciences has demonstrated a commitment to diversity and inclusion. The company has 2,845 employees across its facilities in India, the UK, USA, and Japan. In summary, Sai Life Sciences has made significant progress in sustainability and performance, aligning with its SDGs and demonstrating a strong commitment to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) targets. The company s efforts in reducing emissions, increasing renewable energy usage, and supporting underprivileged communities have set a strong foundation for continued growth and sustainability. <|end|>
