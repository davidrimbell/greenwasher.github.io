IBA reports significant rise in aircraft emissions efficiency post - pandemic

IBA s latest data reveals a significant global decrease in CO2 emissions per Available Seat Kilometre (ASK) from 2019 to 2024, driven by the introduction of more efficient aircraft into airline fleets. The improvement in global aircraft efficiency ranged from 4.1% to 10.1%, with Latin America leading the change at a 10.1% reduction in CO2 emissions per ASK. However, regional variations were observed, with Asia-Pacific recording the smallest improvement of 4.1%. Europe saw a 6.9% reduction in emissions per ASK over the same period. IBA s NetZero platform, including its award-winning IBA NetZero Reporting tool, supports aviation stakeholders in monitoring and reporting sustainability targets with precision and efficiency. The platform offers a wide variety of measurement metrics and enables users to calculate exposures between specific dates accurately. IBA remains dedicated to assisting aviation stakeholders in leveraging its innovative solutions for detailed reporting. <|end|>
