GSMA M360 MENA 2024 Opens Registration For Its First - Ever Doha Edition

The GSMA has announced M360 MENA 2024, a significant event set to take place in Doha, Qatar, from November 18-19, 2024. This event, hosted by the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology of Qatar and Ooredoo, aims to bring together industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators to discuss the future of the MENA region s digital economy, focusing on AI, 5G, and strategic partnerships. The event will feature keynote speeches from prominent figures such as Qatar s Minister of Communications and Information Technology, His Excellency Mohammed bin Ali Al Mannai, GSMA s Director General Mats Granryd, and Ooredoo Qatar s CEO Sheikh Ali Bin Jabor Bin Mohammad Al Thani. They will discuss the transformative power of 5G, AI, and the API economy in shaping the region s digital future. M360 MENA will also showcase collaborations driving digitization across industries, with speakers from various sectors, including sustainability, marketing, and technology. The event will host a range of exhibitors and sponsors, including China Mobile, Comarch, iHorizons, and Protei. The GSMA s M360 series of global events aims to unify the regional mobile ecosystem, discovering, developing, and delivering innovation that serves as the foundation for positive business environments and societal change. The event marks a significant milestone for the MENA region, highlighting its role as a global digital hub and showcasing the transformative potential of AI, 5G, and next-generation technologies. To secure their place, attendees can register to attend the event, which promises to be a platform for exchanging insights, forming digital partnerships, and accelerating innovative technology adoption. The event is expected to drive the digital economy in the MENA region, with predictions of growth from $180 billion to $780 billion by 2030. <|end|>
