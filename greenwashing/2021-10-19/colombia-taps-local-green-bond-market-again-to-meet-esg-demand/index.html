Colombia Taps Local Green Bond Market Again to Meet ESG Demand

Colombia is selling green bonds again, a month after its debut domestic offering handily outperformed in the secondary market amid appetite for securities designed to help the environment. The Andean nation will offer at least 650 billion pesos ($170 million).
