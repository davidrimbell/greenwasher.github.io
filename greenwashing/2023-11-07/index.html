How Companies Are Actually Making a Difference in the Fight Against Greenwashing

Published: 2023-11-07

Companies are tricking people by lying about how environmentally friendly they are. This is called greenwashing and it’s a way for companies to make it seem like they care about the environment when they really don’t. But there are some companies that are actually trying to make a difference.

One example is a company called Twig in the UK. They use robots and artificial intelligence to find better and more sustainable alternatives to chemicals that are used in everyday things. They want to replace ingredients that come from fossil fuels or farming that hurts the environment. Another company, Hoxton Farms, is using technology to make a sustainable type of fat that can be used instead of animal fat.

Even though these solutions are great, there are some problems. The food industry and regulations can make it hard for these ideas to be used. But there are companies like Hektar Real Estate Investment Trust that are being recognized for doing a good job with environmental, social, and governance practices. They have won awards for being great in these areas.

France is also doing something to stop greenwashing. They only let funds use a special label if they don’t invest in companies that make fossil fuels. This makes it easier for people who want to invest in green things to find the right companies. But the financial industry doesn’t like this idea and is fighting against it.

Australia has its own challenges. They want to be a leader in green energy, but it’s hard. They need to spend a lot of money on renewable energy to get rid of emissions by 2050. The government has a plan to help, but they need more money and people with skills in green finance.

In the tourism industry, some companies say they are “carbon neutral” or use “carbon offsets” to show they care about the climate crisis. But it can be hard for travelers to know if these claims are true. Looking for certifications and B Corp certified companies can help travelers make better choices.

Even though greenwashing is a problem, there are companies and things happening that are really trying to make a difference. By staying informed and supporting good practices, people can help make positive changes.
