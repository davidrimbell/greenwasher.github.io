Europe will count natural gas and nuclear as green energy in some circumstances

The European Parliament has voted to keep nuclear and natural gas in the EU’s green classification system, which aims to boost green investment and prevent greenwashing, it has been revealed by the parliament. However, the ruling was rejected.
