Europe scrambles for African gas – DW – 11 / 11 / 2022

Senegal and Mauritania have become the first African countries to supply fossil fuel to Europe, according to the African Union (AU) chief negotiator Mohamed Abdullahi of the UN's climate change summit in Geneva, Switzerland. They are also planning to ship liquefied natural gas to Germany.
