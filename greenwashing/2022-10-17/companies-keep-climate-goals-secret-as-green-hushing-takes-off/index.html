Companies Keep Climate Goals Secret as Green Hushing  Takes Off

Companies are increasingly trying to keep their climate pledges away from public scrutiny. The phenomenon has become pervasive even as businesses set more ambitious internal targets, according to a survey by South Pole.The stigma of so-called greenwashing is so feared that executives will avoid being accused of it.
