Citizen science survey finds 70 % of branded litter traced to a dozen companies | The Wiltshire Gazette and Herald

Coca-Cola, PepsiCo and McDonalds are responsible for most of the drinks and snack brand packaging carrying branding, according to a new report by Citizen Science volunteers in the United States and Canada. Environmentalists have called on the government to change the rules.
