UK Competition Watchdog Takes Aim at Greenwashing in Fashion

The UK’s competition watchdog is investigating environmental claims made by the fashion industry, amid growing concerns about the impact of greenwashing on consumer confidence in their products and a lack of eco-friendly advertising in the sector, it has been revealed.
