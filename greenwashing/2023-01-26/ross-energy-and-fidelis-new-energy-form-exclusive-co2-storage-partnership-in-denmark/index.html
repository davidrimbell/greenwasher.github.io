Ross Energy and Fidelis New Energy form exclusive CO2 storage partnership in Denmark

Energy giant Ross Energy and Fidelis New Energy have entered into an exclusive partnership for the development of onshore CO2 storage networks in Denmark, Southern Sweden and other countries. The project will be completed by 2026, the BBC has learned from the project.
