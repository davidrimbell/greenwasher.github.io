British Fashion Brands Targeted in united kingdom Greenwashing Probe

The UKs competition watchdog has opened an investigation into possible misleading environmental claims made by fashion brands Asos Plc, Boohoo and George at Asda. The Competition and Markets Authority says it has not reached a view as to whether there have been any breaches of consumer protection law.
