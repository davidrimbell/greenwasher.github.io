ASOS , Boohoo and Asda investigated over fashion green claims

ASOS, Boohoo and George are to be asked to take enforcement action if they are using misleading eco-friendly claims at Asda, the competition and markets authority has announced. They will also face courts if it finds the firms’ products are not sustainable.
