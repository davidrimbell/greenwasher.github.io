Telecel Ghana holds sustainability workshop for SMEs

Telecel Ghana has organized an immersive workshop for SMEs, facilitated by Mckingtorch Africa, to promote sustainable business practices. The event, part of the SME Month activities, brought together entrepreneurs from various SMEs, including Landmark Homes and CTA Investments. The workshop focused on integrating Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles into SMEs for long-term success. Makafui Awuku, CEO of Mckingtorch Africa, emphasized the mutual benefits of profitability and sustainability, encouraging SMEs to incorporate sustainable practices as core elements of their businesses. Participants learned how to reduce waste, conserve water, engage host communities, and employ upcycling to drive positive environmental change. The workshop also included hands-on activities, such as creating upcycled products from plastic waste. Attendees, like Patra Agbagbo from Abii National Savings and Loans, expressed their intention to implement sustainable practices in their institutions. The event aims to empower and support the growth of emerging businesses by showcasing the benefits of sustainable practices, including attracting investments and grants.
