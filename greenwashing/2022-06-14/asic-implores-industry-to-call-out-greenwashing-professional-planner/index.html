ASIC implores industry to call out greenwashing - Professional Planner

Greenwashing in financial products is being investigated by the Australian Competition and Investments Regulatory Service (Australian Investment Service) (ASA) in an effort to avoid misleading claims about environmental and social governance and sustainable investment strategy, it has emerged.
