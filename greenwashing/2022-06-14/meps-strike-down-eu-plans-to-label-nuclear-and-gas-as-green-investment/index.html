MEPs strike down EU plans to label nuclear and gas as green investment

The European Parliament has rejected a proposal to label nuclear and fossil gas as sustainable energy sources, saying they do not respect the criteria for environmentally-sustainable economic activities as set out in the Taxonomy Regulation. MEPs have called for the change.
