Cost of living curbs sustainable behaviours in Singapore : Kantar study

Singaporeans are more concerned about the quality and benefits of their sustainable choices, according to a new report from Kantar, the world's leading marketing data firm, in the Asia-Pacific Partnership (PNTR) region. They say the cost of living crisis is preventing them from making sustainability decisions.
