Methanex Releases 2022 Sustainability Report

The world’s biggest producer and supplier of methanol has released a report on its sustainability strategy, which highlights its progress on material environmental, social and governance areas. Environmental safety is among the key themes in the report, with the number of hazard identifications tripled since 2020.
