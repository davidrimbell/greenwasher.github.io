Consumers Beware: How to Spot and Avoid Greenwashing Tactics

Published: 2024-05-09

Some companies use greenwashing to seem eco-friendly. They make false claims to attract green consumers.

Consumers should watch out for vague claims and green imagery. Companies may exaggerate benefits or focus on small things.

Fast fashion brands promote small eco-friendly collections. Some companies use recyclable packaging without addressing their impact.

Consumers should be aware of greenwashing. They can make informed choices and support truly sustainable companies.

Public perception affects greenwashing. Consumers can hold companies accountable and drive positive change.

Volkswagen’s diesel scandal shows the impact of greenwashing. They lost trust by manipulating emissions tests.
