Activists tell EIB to stop greenwashing

The European Investment Bank (EIB) has been accused of funding fossil fuels and highway expansion, according to Greenpeace Luxembourg activists in a demonstration in the capital, Luxembourg, on Saturday. They say the bank is committed to the Paris climate agreement.
