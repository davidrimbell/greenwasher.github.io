iShares ESG Screened S & P Small - Cap ETF ( BATS : XJR ) Shares Up 1 %

The iShares ESG Screened S&P Small-Cap ETF (BATS:XJR) experienced a 1% increase in its share price during mid-day trading on Thursday, reaching a high of $41.50 and closing at $41.41. The trading volume for the day was 18,098 shares. The ETF has a market capitalization of $77.08 million, a PE ratio of 14.40, and a beta of 1.16. Its 50-day moving average price is $40.64, while the 200-day moving average price stands at $38.99. Institutional investors have shown interest in the ETF, with Gould Asset Management LLC CA increasing its stake by 19.4% in the first quarter, owning 8,555 shares worth approximately $335,000. The iShares ESG Screened S&P Small-Cap ETF invests primarily in small-cap stocks that meet sustainability criteria and exclude those with controversial business activities. The fund was launched on September 22, 2020, and is managed by BlackRock.
