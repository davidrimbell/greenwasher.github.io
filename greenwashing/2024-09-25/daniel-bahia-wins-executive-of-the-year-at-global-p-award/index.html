Daniel Bahia Wins Executive of the Year at Global P Award

Daniel Bahia, Head of Global Supply Management at Anglo American, has been recognized with the Executive of the Year Award at The Global Procurement & Supply Chain Awards. His innovative leadership and transformative work in the mining industry have set new standards for procurement and supply chain practices. Bahia s strategy includes global agreements that extend beyond traditional commercial frameworks, focusing on decarbonisation and social and economic development of mining communities. These agreements are expected to generate over US$1.5 billion for Anglo American in the next five years, significantly increasing the value pipeline. Bahia s approach has initiated a shift in how mining companies engage with suppliers and local communities, emphasizing sustainability and social responsibility. Internally, Bahia has implemented a Supply Chain Performance Dashboard, utilizing over 45 million data points to improve performance management and operational excellence. He has also restructured career development and talent management processes, creating clear career progression pathways within the supply chain function. Despite challenges such as post-COVID disruptions and the Russia-Ukraine conflict, Bahia has demonstrated adaptability and a focus on balancing immediate business priorities with long-term value creation. His work showcases how procurement and supply chain management can drive industry-wide transformation towards more sustainable and socially responsible practices.
