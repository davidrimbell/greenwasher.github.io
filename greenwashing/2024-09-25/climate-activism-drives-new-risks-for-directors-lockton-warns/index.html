Climate activism drives new risks for directors , Lockton warns

Lockton, a leading insurance provider, emphasizes the growing importance of various insurance policies, including management liability, directors and officers insurance, professional indemnity, and media liability insurance, in safeguarding against emerging risks. These policies are increasingly vital in the current business landscape, where unforeseen challenges and liabilities can arise. One of the key concerns highlighted by Lockton is the substantial defense costs associated with these risks. In numerous instances, the expenses incurred in defending against claims can surpass the settlement amounts. This discrepancy underscores the need for comprehensive insurance coverage to mitigate potential financial burdens on businesses. Management liability insurance protects companies from claims arising from the actions or decisions of their management team. Directors and officers insurance covers the personal liability of company executives, while professional indemnity insurance safeguards professionals against claims related to errors, omissions, or negligence in their work. Media liability insurance, on the other hand, provides protection for businesses against defamation, privacy breaches, and other media-related risks. The rising significance of these insurance policies is attributed to the evolving nature of business risks. As companies expand and diversify their operations, they become exposed to a broader range of potential liabilities. Moreover, the increasing interconnectedness of global markets and the rapid pace of technological advancements have further amplified the complexity of these risks. In conclusion, Lockton s emphasis on the importance of management liability, directors and officers insurance, professional indemnity, and media liability insurance reflects the growing need for comprehensive risk management strategies in today s business environment. The significant defense costs associated with these risks highlight the necessity for businesses to invest in robust insurance coverage to protect themselves from potential financial setbacks. By doing so, companies can better navigate the complexities of the modern business landscape and ensure their long-term success.
