Brussels to crack down on greenwashing with ban on carbon neutral labels

EU lawmakers have reached an agreement on a new law to ban labelling consumer products as environmental neutral by 2040, which could see companies labelled them as environmentally neutral. These are the latest changes to the EUs greenwashing policy.
