ESG assurance on the rise outside G20

The number of companies in the world receiving assurances for environmental and social engagements has fallen, according to a new report released by the International Federation of Accountants (Fifa) in Geneva. Environmental sustainability assessments are being reviewed in countries outside the G20 summit.
