VictoryShares Core Plus Intermediate Bond ETF ( NASDAQ : UBND ) Increases Dividend to $0 . 09 Per Share

VictoryShares Core Plus Intermediate Bond ETF (NASDAQ:UBND) announced a monthly dividend of 0.0932 per share on September 12th, payable to investors with records on September 11th. This dividend equates to an annualized amount of $1.12 and a yield of 5.01%. The ex-dividend date is September 11th. The ETF s stock experienced a slight decline to $22.33 during mid-day trading on September 11th, with a trading volume of 32,403 shares, lower than its average volume of 51,050. The ETF s 12-month low is $20.18, and its 12-month high is $22.43. The 50-day moving average price is $21.88, and the 200-day moving average price is $21.53. VictoryShares Core Plus Intermediate Bond ETF is an actively managed exchange-traded fund that invests primarily in broad credit fixed income. The fund holds intermediate-term bonds of any type and rating that exhibit positive Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) characteristics. Launched on October 4, 2021, it is managed by VictoryShares.
