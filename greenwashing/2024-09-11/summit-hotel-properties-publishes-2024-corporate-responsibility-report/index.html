SUMMIT HOTEL PROPERTIES PUBLISHES 2024 CORPORATE RESPONSIBILITY REPORT

Summit Hotel Properties, Inc. (NYSE: INN), headquartered in Austin, Texas, has released its annual Corporate Responsibility Report, highlighting the company s dedication to creating long-term shareholder value through responsible investment, environmental protection, and support for employees, communities, and other stakeholders. The report reflects the company s progress in its Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) program and initiatives, with a focus on sustainability and responsibility. Jonathan P. Stanner, President and CEO, expressed his satisfaction with the advancements made in the ESG landscape and the company s commitment to addressing the interests of various stakeholders. Summit Hotel Properties has achieved 87% of its targeted reduction in environmental impact by the end of 2025 and has contributed over $1 million and 1,400 service volunteer hours to support 55 charitable organizations. The company continues to foster a diverse, equitable, and inclusive work culture, rewarding excellence based on merit and performance. Summit Hotel Properties is committed to enhancing its ESG program to align with its values and support its stakeholders. The Corporate Responsibility Report aims to provide stakeholders with a better understanding of the company s commitments to environmental stewardship, social responsibility, governance, and resilience. The report can be accessed in the Responsibility section of the company s website. The press release includes forward-looking statements, which are subject to various risks and uncertainties that could cause actual results to differ materially from those projected. These risks and uncertainties are described in greater detail in the company s filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission, including the Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2023.
