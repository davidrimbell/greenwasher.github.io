$750B fund admin IQ - EQ secures license to offer trust services in Cayman

IQ-EQ, a global investor services group, has expanded its offerings in the Cayman Islands following the granting of a new license by CIMA. This development enables IQ-EQ s Cayman Islands team to provide comprehensive trust services to Ultra High Net Worth (UHNW) families, family offices, and corporations. The Cayman Islands, recognized as one of the top thirty international finance centers, offers a sophisticated professional trust sector, modern trust legislation, and an effective judicial system. Cory Thackeray, Head of Caribbean at IQ-EQ, highlights the significance of this new service offering, emphasizing its ability to meet the growing needs of clients across different industry segments and asset classes. The trust solutions offered by IQ-EQ aim to help clients navigate rapidly changing regulatory landscapes and evolving economic and geopolitical conditions. IQ-EQ s services are supported by a Group-wide commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles and best-in-class technology, including a global data platform and proprietary tools. The company boasts a global workforce of over 5,500 people across 25 jurisdictions and assets under administration exceeding US$750 billion. To learn more about IQ-EQ s services in the Cayman Islands, interested parties can visit the company s website. About IQ-EQ: IQ-EQ is a global, top-tier investor services group that caters to the administration, compliance, and reporting needs of the investment sector worldwide. The company operates as trusted partners to its clients, delivering intelligent solutions through a combination of technical expertise and strong relationships based on deep understanding. IQ-EQ is driven by its Group purpose to power people and possibilities, making life easier, more efficient, and cost-effective for clients, including fund managers, multinational companies, family offices, and private individuals.
