Infosys Collaborates with Clearstream , Part of Deutsche Börse , to Enhance Digital Issuance Platform to Simplify Securities Issuanc

Infosys, a global leader in digital services and consulting, has partnered with Clearstream, a part of Deutsche Börse, to enhance the D7 platform for digital securities issuance. This collaboration has successfully launched the Generation 2 version of the D7 platform, which allows market participants to issue securities digitally. Infosys played a crucial role in implementing, customizing, and deploying the platform, as well as providing go-live and aftercare support. The upgraded D7 platform sets a new standard for the industry by making the digital issuance process faster, easier, and more efficient. Clearstream has already enabled over 150,000 digital issuances through the D7 platform, surpassing any other digital issuance platform globally. Dennis Gada, Executive Vice President and Global Head of Banking & Financial Services at Infosys, expressed delight in collaborating with Clearstream on their digital transformation journey. The integration has empowered Clearstream to improve time-to-market, streamline operations, simplify technology, and manage regulatory changes, fostering sustainable growth. The partnership between Infosys and Clearstream exemplifies the modernization and digitization of the securities and stock exchange industry. The collaboration has successfully accelerated the time-to-market for exchange group s customers and improved their profitability.
