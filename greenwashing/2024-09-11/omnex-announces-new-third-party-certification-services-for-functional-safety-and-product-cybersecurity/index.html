Omnex Announces New Third - Party Certification Services for Functional Safety and Product Cybersecurity

Omnex Safe Secure Sustain (Omnex S3), announced on September 11, 2024, is an independent accredited certification body (CB) established by Omnex Inc. The CB aims to provide global certifications for automotive functional safety, SOTIF, and cybersecurity processes and products. It will also conduct ASPICE audits for Automotive SPICE® V3.1, performed by intacs® certified assessors. Omnex S3 will offer both ANAB-accredited and unaccredited certifications, covering international standards such as ISO 26262 (Automotive Functional Safety), ISO 21448 (SOTIF), ISO/SAE 21434 (Automotive Cybersecurity), and ISO 14064 series (GHG statements and carbon neutrality claims). The organization is committed to promoting safe and secure electric and autonomous vehicles, providing net-zero carbon planning and execution services, and integrating ESG principles and sustainable business practices. Founded in 1985, Omnex Inc. offers consulting, training, and software solutions to enhance performance across management systems and operations. The company serves a diverse range of commercial clients in various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, semiconductor-electronics, e-mobility, medical device, and general manufacturing. Omnex s solutions support critical business functions such as management, operations, supply chain, digitalization, and artificial intelligence. Omnex S3 s certifications and services aim to guide the world and organizations toward a net-zero future defined by healthy, safe, and environmentally sustainable global economies. The organization will verify and/or validate GHG statements and carbon neutrality claims using ISO 14064-3:2019 requirements and references from ISO 14065:2020 and ISO 14066.
