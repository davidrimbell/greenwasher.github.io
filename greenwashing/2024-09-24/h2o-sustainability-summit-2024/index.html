H2O & Sustainability Summit 2024

The H2O & SUSTAINABILITY INNOVATION HUB, an innovation accelerator at the European level, is organizing the H2O & SUSTAINABILITY SUMMIT 2024. This event aims to gather international leaders, experts, academics, and representatives of reference entities to discuss and find practical solutions to contemporary environmental challenges. The summit will address topics such as sustainable water management, water as a strategic vector of security and defense, sustainable energy solutions, the economic value of water, environmental crime, sustainable energy transition, and the role of artificial intelligence in mitigating climate and environmental problems. The H2O & SUSTAINABILITY INNOVATION HUB has several reference partners, including the Municipality of Faro, University of Algarve, Vodafone, and others. The summit will feature lectures, panel discussions, and a Fair Event showcasing innovative projects and solutions from various entities and startups. Telmo Vieira, Managing Partner of PremiValor Consulting and coordinator of the H2O & SUSTAINABILITY INNOVATION HUB, expressed excitement about the participation of 30 countries in the Call for Projects and the summit s potential to create a collaborative space for academics, professionals, and experts to address urgent water-related challenges, sustainability, circular economy, and ESG matters. For more information about the H2O & SUSTAINABILITY SUMMIT 2024, including program details and registration, interested parties can visit the H2O & SUSTAINABILITY INNOVATION HUB website at www.h2o-sustainability-hub.com.
