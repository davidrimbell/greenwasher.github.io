Societe Generale : Report form for 23 / 09 / 2024

Societe Generale, a leading European bank, has over 126,000 employees and serves approximately 25 million clients in 65 countries. With a history spanning 160 years, the bank offers a diverse range of advisory and financial solutions to corporate, institutional, and individual clients. The Group operates through three main business segments: French Retail, Private Banking and Insurance, Global Banking and Investor Solutions, and Mobility, International Retail Banking and Financial Services. The bank s commitment to sustainable value creation is evident in its ESG offerings for all clients, including retail banking, private banking, and investor solutions. Societe Generale is also a prominent player in sustainable mobility, with Ayvens as its global brand. The Group s dedication to environmental transition and sustainability is further demonstrated by its inclusion in several socially responsible investment indices, such as DJSI (Europe), FTSE4Good (Global and Europe), and MSCI Low Carbon Leaders Index (World and Europe). For more information, Societe Generale can be followed on Twitter/X @societegenerale or by visiting their website at societegenerale.com.
