Amundi - Acba Launches Academy to Enhance Financial Literacy and Professional Development - aysor . am

The Amundi-Acba Academy, launched by Amundi-Acba Asset Management, aims to foster financial literacy and professional growth in Armenia. This initiative, inspired by the French Amundi Academy and European best practices, offers a range of online training modules focused on finance, asset management, and investment strategies. The Academy provides four learning directions, including fundamental topics and specialized trainings in CFA and ESG principles. Key features of the Amundi-Acba Academy include: 1. Four Directions of Learning: The Academy covers fundamental and expert topics, offering specialized trainings in CFA and ESG principles. 2. Free Access to Local Students: Local students will have free access to courses and opportunities to participate in the Academy programs. 3. Collaborative Partnerships: Amundi-Acba will establish partnerships with Armenian universities, granting them access to valuable training modules. 4. Localized Research Center: The company will release articles in Armenian from the Amundi Investment Institute, covering topics such as geopolitics, macroeconomics, and financial markets. The launch of the Amundi-Acba Academy aligns with the company s mission to empower students and individuals through education and contribute to the improvement of Armenia s financial ecosystem. Amundi-Acba Asset Management, founded in 2013, manages three mandatory pension funds with over 517 billion assets under management as of August 30th, 2024. The company s shareholders include the French Amundi and Acba Bank, a major financial institution in Armenia. In summary, the Amundi-Acba Academy is a comprehensive educational initiative designed to enhance financial literacy and professional development in Armenia. It offers a range of online training modules, free access to local students, collaborative partnerships with universities, and localized research content. The program aims to empower individuals and contribute to a more robust and transparent financial landscape in Armenia.
