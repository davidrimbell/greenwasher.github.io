Broker Expo 2024 Countdown : Jonathan Evans of Sustain Insurance Brokers

Broker Week, organized by Insurance Age, is a significant event for the insurance broking sector, offering a week of activities to promote the industry. Jonathan Evans, founder of Sustain Insurance Brokers, shares his insights and experiences from the inaugural event and his upcoming session at the Broker Expo 2024. Evans emphasizes the importance of convincing clients that green extends beyond their golf courses, staying updated on regulatory changes, and even caring for an office plant as a form of environmental action. His main takeaway from the first Broker Week was the unexpected entertainment value of bringing together industry professionals. In his upcoming session, Evans will focus on making Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) topics profitable for brokers, beyond superficial measures like recycling bins. He also highlights the impressive innovation of AI technology that translates insurance jargon into plain English, though he still seeks a solution for explaining policies to non-experts. Evans favorite chocolate bar is a Twirl, symbolizing the complexity and satisfaction of insurance work. He also recalls attending a gardening show at the Birmingham NEC, which was less exciting than Broker Expo.
