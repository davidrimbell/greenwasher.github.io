Global New Material International : Fulfilling Social Responsibilities to Create a More Colorful World - Iberonews

Global New Material International Holdings Limited (GNMI), a Chinese company specializing in high-quality appearance color solutions, was recognized at the first Sino-European Corporate ESG Best Practice Conference in Frankfurt, Germany. The company received the Best Social Responsibility Case award for its commitment to corporate culture based on integrity, innovation, leadership, and harmony. GNMI s pearlescent materials are used in various industries, including cosmetics, automotive coatings, plastics, inks, textiles, printing, ceramics, and ship corrosion prevention. These materials not only enhance product appearance but also improve performance. Su Ertian, Chairman of the Board of GNMI, emphasized the company s dedication to integrating high-quality, green, and sustainable development into its future strategy. GNMI has actively fulfilled its social responsibilities through sustainable development strategies, social welfare participation, and corporate social responsibility. The company contributes to community initiatives, donates funds and materials, and participates in charitable projects. Receiving the Best Social Responsibility Case award highlights GNMI s significant contributions to promoting social and environmental sustainability. The company s achievements in employee care, social development, and public welfare participation are also recognized. Su concluded that GNMI will continue to adhere to its long-term economic values and social responsibilities, striving to better fulfill its environmental, social, and governance duties while creating more beautiful colors for the world.
