Matrix Service Company Publishes Fiscal 2024 Sustainability

Matrix Service Company, a leading contractor in the energy and industrial markets across North America, released its Fiscal 2024 Sustainability Report. The report highlights the company s commitment to sustainability, operational excellence, and financial performance. Key achievements include the completion of the company s first Materiality Assessment, which helps prioritize sustainability efforts and meet stakeholder expectations. The assessment also focuses on enhancing project management, compliance, and GHG emissions reporting. Matrix Service Company has adopted the Sustainability Accounting Standards Board (SASB) reporting standards and the Financial Stability Board’s Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosure (TCFD) recommendations. This approach aims to provide consistent, comparable, and meaningful reporting on sustainability information. The company s sustainability efforts are embedded in its core values, with a focus on building strong Environment, Social, and Governance (ESG) practices. Matrix Service Company has been recognized for its board diversification, CEO Action for Diversity and Inclusion signatory status, and Great Place to Work® recognition. The complete report is available on the company s Investor Relations website, and further information can be found at www.matrixservicecompany.com. The release contains forward-looking statements, which are subject to risks and uncertainties that may cause actual results to differ from current expectations. The company s future performance will depend on various economic, competitive, and other influences, as discussed in the Risk Factors and Forward Looking Statements sections of the report. For more information, contact Kellie Smythe, Senior Director of Investor Relations, Marketing, Communications, and Sustainability at Matrix Service Company.
