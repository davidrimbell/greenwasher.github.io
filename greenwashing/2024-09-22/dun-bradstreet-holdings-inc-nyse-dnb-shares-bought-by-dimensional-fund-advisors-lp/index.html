Dun & Bradstreet Holdings , Inc . ( NYSE : DNB ) Shares Bought by Dimensional Fund Advisors LP

Dimensional Fund Advisors LP increased its stake in Dun & Bradstreet Holdings, Inc. by 14.1% in Q2, owning 14.66 million shares worth $135.8M. Other investors, including the State Treasurer of North Carolina and OLD National Bancorp IN, also adjusted their holdings. Hedge funds and institutional investors own 86.68% of the company s stock. Wall Street analysts have mixed views, with a consensus rating of Moderate Buy and an average target price of $13.85. Dun & Bradstreet reported $0.23 EPS for Q2, up 3.9% YoY, with a revenue of $576.2M. The company announced a $0.05 quarterly dividend, yielding 1.78%. Dun & Bradstreet offers business-to-business data and analytics, with products like D&B Finance Analytics and D&B Risk Analytics.
