Invesco Real Assets ESG ETF ( BATS : IVRA ) Raises Dividend to $0 . 04 Per Share

Invesco Real Assets ESG ETF (IVRA) announced a dividend of $0.0352 per share on September 27th for shareholders of record on September 23rd. This represents an increase from the previous dividend of $0.02. The ETF, which invests in total market equity of North American real assets companies with ESG and fundamental factors, opened at $16.30 on September 20th. With a market cap of $5.87 million, a price-to-earnings ratio of 19.04, and a beta of 0.88, IVRA s 50-day moving average price is $15.51, and its two-hundred day moving average price is $14.50. Invesco Real Assets ESG ETF is an actively managed, non-transparent fund utilizing the Fidelity model.
