Al Maha Petroleum wins Best Finance Team of the Year award

Al Maha Petroleum Products Marketing Company s finance team has been recognized as the Best Finance Team of the Year at the Oman CFO Forum, highlighting their exceptional contributions to financial performance, strategic growth, and operational excellence. The team s dedication to achieving financial targets, maximizing net profits, and enhancing share prices has set them apart in the Oman fuel marketing industry. The team s focus on profit growth, cost-saving measures, and operational efficiency has been instrumental in their success. Their commitment to innovation and efficiency, particularly through digital transformation, has led to significant performance improvements. The finance team has been at the forefront of Al Maha s digital transformation efforts, integrating advanced technologies like Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and cloud-based financial systems. This proactive approach has ensured the company s adaptability in a rapidly evolving market, solidifying its position as an industry leader. Beyond financial metrics, the finance team has played a crucial role in Al Maha s strategic initiatives, such as market expansion and product diversification. Their guidance in launching high-margin product lines and non-fuel retail projects has bolstered the company s revenue and stability. The team s dedication to collaboration and team development has fostered a strong work culture and continuous growth. Hiriyanna Narayanaswamy, Al Maha s Chief Financial Officer, expressed his excitement and gratitude for the prestigious award. He highlighted the team s leadership in finance acumen, risk management, compliance, and a proactive approach to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) initiatives. This recognition serves as a testament to the team s dedication and excellence in sustainable and ethical business practices. In summary, Al Maha s finance team has been honored for their outstanding contributions to financial performance, strategic growth, and operational excellence. Their commitment to innovation, digital transformation, and sustainable business practices has solidified the company s position as an industry leader in the Oman fuel marketing industry.
