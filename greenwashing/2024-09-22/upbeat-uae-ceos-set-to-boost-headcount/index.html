Upbeat UAE CEOs set to boost headcount

The KPMG UAE CEO Outlook Report 2024 highlights a positive outlook for the UAE s economy, with CEOs expressing more confidence in growth prospects than their global counterparts. The report, based on a survey of 1,325 CEOs worldwide, reveals that 96% of UAE CEOs are confident in their industry s growth, compared to 79% in the Middle East. This optimism is attributed to the UAE s stable economy, supportive business environment, and government focus on innovation and sustainability. The report also notes that 72% of CEOs globally are confident in economic growth, with 82% of UAE CEOs expecting the nation s economy to improve in 2024. The UAE s diversification strategies, particularly in knowledge-based industries, contribute to this positive outlook. In terms of workforce development, 68% of UAE CEOs believe that investment in skills development and lifelong learning is crucial for securing future talent pipelines. The emphasis on Emiratisation is expected to drive the private sector forward. AI adoption and ESG investments remain priorities for UAE CEOs, with 52% considering generative AI a top investment priority in 2024. Additionally, 56% of UAE CEOs anticipate returns on ESG investments within 3 to 5 years, aligning with the country s sustainability agenda and global trends in corporate strategies. Despite the positive outlook, CEOs globally and locally anticipate balancing centralized and devolved strategies in response to geopolitical uncertainties and climate change. In summary, the KPMG UAE CEO Outlook Report 2024 reflects a bullish outlook for the UAE s economy, driven by a stable business environment, government support for innovation and sustainability, and a focus on workforce development and Emiratisation. CEOs in the UAE are confident in their industry s growth and are prioritizing investments in AI and ESG initiatives to shape future growth.
