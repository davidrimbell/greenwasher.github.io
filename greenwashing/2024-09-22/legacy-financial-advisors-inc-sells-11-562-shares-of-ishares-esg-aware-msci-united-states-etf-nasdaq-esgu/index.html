Legacy Financial Advisors Inc . Sells 11 , 562 Shares of iShares ESG Aware MSCI united states ETF ( NASDAQ : ESGU )

Legacy Financial Advisors Inc. significantly reduced its position in iShares ESG Aware MSCI USA ETF (ESGU) by 81.5% in the second quarter, selling 11,562 shares and leaving 2,629 shares worth $314,000. Conversely, several other institutional investors adjusted their stakes in the fund. Brighton Jones LLC increased its holdings by 6.2%, owning 2,509 shares valued at $299,000. Blair William & Co. IL boosted its shares by 8.7%, now holding 12,482 shares worth $1,489,000. Cranbrook Wealth Management LLC saw a 37.6% increase, owning 9,235 shares valued at $1,102,000. The iShares ESG Aware MSCI USA ETF experienced a slight decrease of 0.3% with shares opening at $125.36 on Friday. The fund has a market cap of $13.29 billion, a P/E ratio of 25.85, and a beta of 1.02. The 52-week low and high are $89.69 and $126.09, respectively. The ETF, managed by BlackRock, tracks the MSCI USA Extended ESG Focus index, focusing on companies with positive environmental, social, and governance characteristics.
