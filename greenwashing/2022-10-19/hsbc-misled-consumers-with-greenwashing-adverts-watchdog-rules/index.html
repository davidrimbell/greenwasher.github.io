HSBC misled consumers with greenwashing adverts , watchdog rules

HSBC has been banned from publishing environmental adverts for the first time in more than a decade, the Advertising Standards Authority has said. They appeared in Bristol and London last year, which claimed it would provide $1 trillion in financing for clients to transition to net zero.
