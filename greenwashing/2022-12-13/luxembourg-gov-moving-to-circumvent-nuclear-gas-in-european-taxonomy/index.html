Luxembourg gov moving to circumvent nuclear & gas in European taxonomy

The European Parliament has passed a draft amendment to the 2021 budget, which aims to exclude nuclear and gas from the tax advantage of the reduced rate for investment funds in Luxembourg, as part of efforts to promote economic activities considered sustainable.
