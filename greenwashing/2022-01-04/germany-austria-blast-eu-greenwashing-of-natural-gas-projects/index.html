Germany , Austria blast EU greenwashing of natural gas projects

A row has broken out between Germany and Austria over a proposal to classify renewable energy projects as sustainable investments, according to reports from the Bloomberg news agency. Both countries have accused the bloc of “greenwashing” and threatening to divert investment away from nuclear power.
