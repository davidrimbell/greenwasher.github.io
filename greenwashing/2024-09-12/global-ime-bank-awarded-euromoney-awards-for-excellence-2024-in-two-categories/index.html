Global IME Bank Awarded Euromoney Awards For Excellence 2024 In Two Categories

Global IME Bank Limited has been recognized as Nepal s Best Bank and Nepal’s Best Bank for ESG among Nepali Banks and Financial Institutions (BFIs) in the Euromoney Awards for Excellence 2024. The bank s Chairman, Chandra Prasad Dhakal, and CEO, Surendra Raj Regmi, received the awards at a special function in Singapore. The Euromoney Award, established in 1992, is a prestigious accolade in the global banking industry, honoring outstanding performance. This recognition is not the first for Global IME Bank, as it previously received the Euromoney Award for Excellence 2022 - Best Bank of Nepal. The bank has also received various other prestigious awards, including Bank of the Year 2014, Best Internet Bank 2016, Best Bank Award 2024, and Best Employer Award. Global IME Bank is the first private sector bank in Nepal with a branch network in all 77 districts, boasting over 1,100 service centers, including 354 branch offices, 384 ATMs, 255 branchless banking services, 68 extension and revenue collection counters, and 3 foreign representative offices. In addition to serving Nepali citizens, the bank facilitates inward remittances from various countries worldwide, including the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Malaysia, South Korea, Japan, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, UAE, Bahrain, Kuwait, India, Jordan, and Hong Kong. Global IME Bank is committed to continuing its exemplary banking services and upholding its reputation as a leading financial institution in Nepal.
