Schneider Electric Launches Zeigo™ Activate Lite , an Enhanced Version of its Complimentary Subscription Plan for Comprehensive Emissions Management

Schneider Electric, a global leader in energy management and automation, has enhanced Zeigo Activate, a part of its decarbonization SaaS platform. The new Zeigo Activate Lite version offers features to help businesses establish carbon emissions baselines, set decarbonization targets, calculate Scope 1 and 2 emissions, and connect with solution providers for renewable energy and efficiency goals. This freemium version is now available for new users at no cost, emphasizing accessibility and sustainability for organizations of all sizes. Large corporations can also promote Zeigo Activate Lite within their value chain, encouraging suppliers and partners to use the platform to address Scope 3 emissions and better understand their total emissions portfolio. The new version includes digital educational resources, a network of solution providers, and an intuitive design for ease of use. For those seeking additional features, Zeogo Activate s Pro version offers simplified uploading tools, reporting tools, Scope 3 emissions calculation, a customized roadmap, peer benchmarking, unlimited users, and support for multiple sites. Laura Eve, VP of SaaS Sustainability Solutions at Schneider Electric, highlights the importance of Zeogo Activate Lite in a world where emission reduction is increasingly driven by legislation, compliance, and customer demand. The platform s intuitive design makes it simple and effective for businesses to calculate and reduce emissions. Zeogo Activate Lite is a significant advancement for companies entering the decarbonization space, providing a wide range of tools to help businesses take tangible steps towards reducing their environmental impact. The platform has been recognized by Environment + Energy Leader as the 2024 Product – Software & Cloud Award Winner, underscoring Schneider Electric s commitment to environmental sustainability and energy management.
