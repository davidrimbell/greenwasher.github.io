Assurant Included on TIME World Best Companies 2024 R ...

Assurant, Inc., a global business services company, has been recognized in the TIME World s Best Companies 2024 ranking. The ranking, conducted by TIME and Statista, evaluates companies based on employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability (ESG). Assurant s inclusion in the ranking reflects its strong performance in these areas. The company, headquartered in Atlanta, has a presence in 21 countries and is a Fortune 500 company. Assurant specializes in supporting major consumer purchases, including mobile devices, auto, and housing markets. The company partners with leading brands to develop innovative solutions and deliver an enhanced customer experience. Assurant s success is attributed to its culture, which empowers the team and fosters a purpose-driven organization. The company s commitment to sustainable growth and innovation is evident in its various products, such as mobile device solutions, extended service contracts, vehicle protection services, renters insurance, and lender-placed insurance products. Keith Demmings, president and CEO of Assurant, expressed pride in the company s inclusion in the TIME World s Best Companies ranking. He emphasized the strength of the team and the shared commitment to purpose as key factors in the company s ongoing success. Assurant s recognition in the TIME World s Best Companies 2024 ranking highlights its strong performance in employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability. The company s focus on innovation and customer experience, along with its global presence, positions it as a leading player in the business services industry.
