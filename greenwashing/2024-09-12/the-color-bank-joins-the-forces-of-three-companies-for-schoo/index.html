The educational community of the largest municipality in Attica, Mandras-Edylia, received support from three Greek companies, Vitex, Endless EC, and Frameworks Computers S.A., to enhance school facilities for the new school year. Vitex provided 1,000 liters of color to upgrade schoolrooms, while Endless EC donated cleaning and paper products for the entire school year. Frameworks Computers S.A. contributed modern electronic equipment and graphics, creating a well-equipped educational space. The CEOs of these companies, Mr. The present presence of representatives from Vitex, Endless EC, and Frameworks Computers S.A. is a practical proof of their support to the local society. Their donations not only improve school infrastructure but also contribute to offering children a better educational environment. This cooperation is vital for the region s development and the quality of life of its citizens. Vitex, a leading company in Greece in the field of paints and construction materials, operates with sustainability principles, including zero waste and net zero energy practices. Endless EC, a Greek company active in the paper and detergent industry, is a member of the GREECE-OUR initiative, a community of Greek productive companies. Frameworks Computers S.A., established in 1969, is a leader in the Greek technology industry, with a focus on creating value and supporting evolution in the technology sector. These partnerships between local government and Greek businesses demonstrate that when there is a will for the common good, there is also a way to achieve it. The combined efforts of these companies have significantly contributed to the improvement of educational facilities in the Municipality of Mandras-Edylia.
