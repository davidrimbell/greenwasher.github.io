New England Private Wealth Advisors LLC Purchases 3 , 439 Shares of JPMorgan Nasdaq Equity Premium Income ETF ( NASDAQ : JEPQ )

New England Private Wealth Advisors LLC increased its stake in JPMorgan Nasdaq Equity Premium Income ETF (NASDAQ:JEPQ) by 3.1% during the second quarter, owning 115,829 shares worth $6,429,000. The ETF represents 1.1% of the firm s portfolio, making it its 29th largest holding. Other significant investors also adjusted their positions, with Beacon Capital Management and SJS Investment Consulting Inc. purchasing new shares valued at $26,000 and $28,000, respectively. Global Wealth Strategies & Associates boosted its holdings by 36.4%, owning 750 shares worth $41,000. Thurston Springer Miller Herd & Titak Inc. also acquired shares valued at $56,000. JPMorgan Nasdaq Equity Premium Income ETF opened at $53.16 on Thursday, with a 52-week low of $44.95 and a high of $56.18. The ETF has a market cap of $15.09 billion, a P/E ratio of 32.10, and a beta of -0.84. The fund recently announced a monthly dividend of $0.5569 per share, payable on September 5th, representing an annualized dividend of $6.68 and a yield of 12.57%. The ETF invests in large-cap US companies from the Nasdaq-100 Index, using ESG factors and a proprietary data science-driven investment approach.
