Spectral Capital Announces Quantum Bridge Advisory Board to Lead Quantum Computing and Decentralized Cloud Innovation

Spectral Capital Corporation (OTCQB:FCCN), a leader in quantum computing and decentralized cloud technologies, has announced the formation of its Quantum Bridge Advisory Board. This expert group will guide Spectral s Quantum Bridge Initiative, which aims to revolutionize data, decentralized cloud infrastructure, and quantum computing technologies across 16 global regions and 251 nations. The initiative will develop Spectral s Quantum-as-a-Service (QaaS) platform, integrating quantum technology with energy-efficient, sustainable data centers. The Quantum Bridge Advisory Board will be crucial in creating an infrastructure that is secure, scalable, and environmentally friendly, using Green Wave Technology and the Distributed Quantum Ledger Database (DQLDB). Spectral s global strategy aligns with the MSCI Global Industry Classification Standard (GICS) taxonomy, targeting growth opportunities in 11 GICS sectors. Investors should take note of Spectral s commitment to sustainability and ESG principles, as it develops eco-friendly data centers. The company invites nominations for the Quantum Bridge Advisory Board and encourages interested parties to submit their names. Spectral Capital s CEO, Jenifer Osterwalder, emphasizes the company s dedication to shaping the future of data in computing. The initiative will address real-world challenges and create significant growth and value for stakeholders. For more information, visit Spectral Capital s website.
