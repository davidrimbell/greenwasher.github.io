Inmarsat : oil , gas firms share ESG data with investors more than other sectors

Oil and gas firms are more willing to share environmental, social and governance-focused data with investors than any other commercial land-based industry, in the face of mounting pressure from the industry to prove they are working to tackle climate change, according to new research.
