Industry Lobby The US Government to Make Africa Backslide on Plastics

Greenpeace has called for the US to phase out single-use plastic waste across Africa, according to documents obtained by a US-Kenyan chemical lobby group which counts Shell, Exxon, Total and Total among its members. Environmentalists have warned that they could derail the progress made in the continent.
