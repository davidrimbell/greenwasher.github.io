Tackling Sustainability And Social Impact Issues Through Critical Dialogue At 3BL Network Effect : San Jose

Silicon Valley companies focused on sustainability and social impact will convene at the 3BL Network Effect: San Jose event on October 23. The half-day program, hosted by Dave Armon and taking place at Logitech s campus, welcomes professionals in corporate social responsibility, sustainability, social impact, communications, marketing, and investor relations from various organizations. Breakfast will be provided, and the event is free to attend. The agenda includes discussions on the impact of sustainability-driven brand switching, which amounts to $44 billion across 12 U.S. industries. The event will also feature a fireside chat on the evolution of ESG strategy and reporting practices at Intuit, led by Jeff Rangel and Peggy Brannigan. Additionally, Nicole Johnson and Kendall Arthur from Cadence Design Systems and Fast Forward will discuss Cadence s Fem approach, which aims to promote gender equality in the tech industry. Wendy Ng and Stacey Faucett from Cisco Systems will share circular design principles and the power of storytelling in sustainability efforts. Logitech s initiatives, such as the Aurora Product Collection and the Adaptive E-Sports Tournament, will be highlighted to showcase their commitment to inclusivity and equity in gaming. Attendees can register for the event at no cost, with breakfast included. The event aims to foster networking and partnerships among Silicon Valley companies, nonprofits, and NGOs to create a more impactful future.
