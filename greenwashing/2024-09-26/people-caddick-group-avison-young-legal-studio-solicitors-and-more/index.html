People : Caddick Group ; Avison Young ; Legal Studio Solicitors ; and more

West Yorkshire-based Caddick has appointed Jessica Herman as its new head of ESG and sustainability, leading the company s Places for Life strategy. Herman, previously with JustOne UK and JLL, brings over eight years of experience in sustainability and real estate. Caddick s strategy aims to create the best places in the country, supporting people and the environment. Herman will collaborate with senior leaders and the ESG Taskforce to govern the strategy. Meanwhile, Avison Young has promoted Jill Goodman to MD of its lease advisory team, overseeing a team of 11 and new initiatives for growth and sustainability. Goodman s experience in market shifts will help the team deliver the best outcomes for clients. Legal Studio has welcomed Philip Williams as a consultant solicitor specializing in Private Client. Williams brings experience from Addleshaw Goddard, Wrigleys Solicitors, and Harrowells Solicitors. Ian McCann, CEO of Legal Studio, is excited to have Williams join the team and contribute to the company s Private Client offering. Lastly, Stafforce has appointed Danny Marshall as operations manager for its Ports division. Marshall will drive operational excellence and support new site launches, troubleshooting challenges, and implementing best practices. Tim Platt, brand director, is confident Marshall s expertise will help Stafforce Ports become the largest supplier of port labor in the UK.
