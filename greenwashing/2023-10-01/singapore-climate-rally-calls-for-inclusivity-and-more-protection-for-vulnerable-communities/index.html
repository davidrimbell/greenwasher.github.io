Singapore climate rally calls for inclusivity and more protection for vulnerable communities

Singapore has held a climate rally in the capital, Hong Lim Park, to promote justice for the environmental impacts of rising levels of greenhouse gas emissions, according to the city’s sustainability agency Eco-business. Climate activists have urged residents to take stronger action to reduce the risks.
