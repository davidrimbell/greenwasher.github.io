Brazil Amazon deforestation climbed 22 % in a year : NPR

The Brazilian Amazon rainforest has lost its highest annual rate of deforestation in more than 15 years, according to new data published by the country's space research agency (CNSA). Scientists believe that the region has seen its worst loss since 2006.
