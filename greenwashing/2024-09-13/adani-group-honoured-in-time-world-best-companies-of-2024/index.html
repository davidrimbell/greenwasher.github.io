Adani Group Honoured In TIME World Best Companies Of 2024

Adani Group, a leading integrated business conglomerate, has been featured in TIME magazine s World s Best Companies of 2024 list, prepared in collaboration with Statista. Eight out of eleven portfolio companies were considered in the evaluation, showcasing the group s comprehensive performance. The recognized companies include Adani Enterprises Ltd, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd, Adani Green Energy Ltd, Adani Energy Solutions Ltd, Adani Total Gas Ltd, Ambuja Cements, Adani Power Ltd, and Adani Wilmar Ltd. The accolade highlights the Adani Group s commitment to employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability. The company s net profit increased by 50.1% to INR 10,279 crore in the first quarter of the fiscal year, while EBITDA reached INR 22,570 crore, up 32.9% year-on-year. The World s Best Companies 2024 list is based on a rigorous analysis across three key dimensions: employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability (ESG). The Adani Group, India s largest and fastest-growing diversified business portfolio, has interests across energy and utilities, transport and logistics, natural resources, and consumer sectors. The group s emerging businesses, including solar and wind manufacturing, airports, and roads, have contributed to its success. This recognition further validates the Adani Group s hard work and continuous efforts to push boundaries and deliver excellence across its businesses.
