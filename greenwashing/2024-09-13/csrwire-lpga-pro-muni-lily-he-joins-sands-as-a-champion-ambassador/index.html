CSRWire - LPGA Pro Muni Lily He Joins Sands as a Champion Ambassador

Las Vegas Sands (NYSE: LVS) announced that LPGA Tour pro Muni Lily He, a China-born golfer with a decorated junior career, will join their roster of champion ambassadors. He will work with Sands to promote women s golf and highlight women s leadership on and off the golf course through appearances and talks. Born in Chengdu, China, and currently residing in Los Angeles, He has a significant following on Instagram and has achieved notable success in her professional golf career, including securing her LPGA Tour Card in 2020. Sands Chairman and CEO Robert G. Goldstein sees Lily as a valuable addition to their team, particularly in Asia, where she can serve as an inspirational role model. The company s commitment to corporate responsibility and sustainability is also highlighted, with inclusion on the Dow Jones Sustainability Indices for World and North America. For more information, visit www.sands.com.
