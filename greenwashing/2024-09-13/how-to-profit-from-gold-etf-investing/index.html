How to Profit From Gold ETF Investing

Gold ETF investing offers a way to participate in the gold market s potential rally without directly investing in individual stocks. The SPDR Gold Shares ETF (GLD) is a popular choice, providing exposure to gold through physical bullion stored in vaults. It s easy to buy and sell, with a 24% increase in 2024. Other options include the ProShares Ultra Gold ETF (UGL), which delivers two times the daily performance of gold prices through exposure to gold futures. However, it carries higher risk due to its leverage. The Franklin Responsibly Sourced Gold ETF (FGDL) offers exposure to gold miners that adhere to ESG standards. For investors interested in smaller mining companies with big upside potential, the VanEck Junior Gold Miners ETF (GDXJ) is a good choice. It provides exposure to smaller companies like Kinross Gold Corporation and Pan American Silver Corp. For those seeking leverage, the Direxion Daily Gold Miners Index Bull 2x Shares ETF (NUGT) offers two times the daily performance of the VanEck Gold Miners ETF. However, it s riskier and may be best for more experienced investors. Overall, gold ETF investing provides a variety of options for investors to gain exposure to the gold market. With gold potentially setting up for a massive supercycle, it s essential for investors to consider gold ETF investing as part of their portfolio.
