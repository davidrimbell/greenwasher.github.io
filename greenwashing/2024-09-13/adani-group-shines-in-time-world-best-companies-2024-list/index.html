Adani Group Shines in TIME World Best Companies 2024 List

The Adani Group has been recognized as one of the World s Best Companies of 2024 by TIME, in collaboration with Statista. This acknowledgment reflects the group s commitment to employee satisfaction, revenue growth, and sustainability. The selection process involved a comprehensive analysis of three key dimensions: Employee Satisfaction, Revenue Growth, and Sustainability (ESG). Employee satisfaction was assessed through surveys conducted in over 50 countries, with around 170,000 participants providing insights on work conditions, salary, equality, and overall company image. Companies were required to demonstrate significant revenue growth, with revenues exceeding USD 100 million in 2023 and showcasing their growth trajectory from 2021 to 2023. Sustainability evaluations were based on standardized ESG KPIs from Statista s ESG Database and targeted research. Eight of the eleven Adani Group portfolio companies were highlighted in this assessment, indicating strong performance across the group. These companies include Adani Enterprises Ltd, Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd, Adani Green Energy Ltd, Adani Energy Solutions Ltd, Adani Total Gas Ltd, Ambuja Cements Ltd, Adani Power Ltd, and Adani Wilmar Ltd. The Adani Group, headquartered in Ahmedabad, is India s largest and fastest-growing diversified business portfolio, with interests in energy, utilities, transport, logistics, natural resources, and consumer sectors. This recognition by TIME and Statista underscores the group s dedication to excellence and sustainable growth in various business sectors. (Source: ANI) (With inputs from agencies.)
