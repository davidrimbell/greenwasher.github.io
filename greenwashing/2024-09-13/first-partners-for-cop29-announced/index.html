First partners for COP29 announced

The first official Green Zone partners for COP29 in Azerbaijan have been announced, including Azerbaijani businesses SOCAR Green, PASHA Holding, and Azersun Holding. These companies have committed to sustainable practices and global climate goals, engaging with Azerbaijan’s National Pledge Programme to measure their potential to meet climate objectives and formulate future ESG strategies. The Green Zone partners will use this platform to connect with global climate policies, showcase their sustainability efforts, and support climate action transition in their business areas. The Green Zone will host a diverse mix of organizations showcasing climate-friendly products and solutions, with a full schedule of focused programs, including presentations, networking events, workshops, briefings, and music. Businesses can also host their own events with access to conference booking facilities and various meeting room formats for collaboration and networking. Narmin Jarchalova, Chief Operating Officer for COP29 Azerbaijan, emphasized the importance of the Green Zone partners in advancing climate action and fostering global cooperation. Elmir Musayev, General Director of SOCAR Green, highlighted their commitment to leading Azerbaijan s transition to a sustainable future. Mir Jamal Pashayev, Deputy Chairman of the Board of Directors, PASHA Holding, expressed their eagerness to engage with global leaders and stakeholders. Abdolbari Gozal, Chairperson of the Supervisory Board of Azersun Holding, emphasized their unwavering commitment to sustainability and the potential for meaningful change through collaboration with global leaders. Further opportunities to participate and partner with the Green Zone at COP29 in Azerbaijan are still available, with more information available on the COP29 Green Zone website.
