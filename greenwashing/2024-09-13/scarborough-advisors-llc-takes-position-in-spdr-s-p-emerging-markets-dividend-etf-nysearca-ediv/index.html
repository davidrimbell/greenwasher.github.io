Scarborough Advisors LLC Takes Position in SPDR S & P Emerging Markets Dividend ETF ( NYSEARCA : EDIV )

Scarborough Advisors LLC, Continuum Advisory LLC, Envestnet Portfolio Solutions Inc., ESG Planning DBA Harper Investing, and UBS Group AG have recently adjusted their positions in SPDR S&P Emerging Markets Dividend ETF (NYSEARCA:EDIV), with varying degrees of increase in their holdings. Scarborough Advisors LLC acquired 859 shares valued at approximately $30,000 in the 2nd quarter. Continuum Advisory LLC increased its shares by 27.2%, owning 4,279 shares worth $151,000. Envestnet Portfolio Solutions Inc. raised its holdings by 2.6%, now owning 12,155 shares valued at $395,000. ESG Planning DBA Harper Investing boosted its position by 155.6%, owning 28,798 shares worth $1,017,000. UBS Group AG increased its holdings by 1.2%, now owning 271,142 shares valued at $8,818,000. Nations Financial Group Inc. IA ADV also acquired a new stake in the ETF in the fourth quarter, valued at about $986,000. SPDR S&P Emerging Markets Dividend ETF, a high dividend yield equity ETF, tracks a yield-weighted index of high-dividend-paying firms in emerging markets. The fund has a market capitalization of $430.35 million, a PE ratio of 8.45, and a beta of 0.62. Its 50-day simple moving average is $35.78, and its 200-day simple moving average is $34.88.
