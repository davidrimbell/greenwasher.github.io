CGI completes acquisition of Aeyon , expands capabilities for national security and civilian agency missions

CGI Federal Inc., a subsidiary of CGI Inc., has completed the acquisition of Aeyon, a company specializing in digital transformation and AI technologies for the U.S. Federal Government. This move enhances CGI s service offerings and strengthens its relationships with key U.S. national security clients, including the Office of the Secretary of Defense and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). The acquisition aligns with CGI s values and commitment to excellence, as stated by CGI President, Stephanie Mango. Aeyon Founder and CEO, Sunny Singh, also expressed enthusiasm for joining CGI s experienced team and the company s culture. Aeyon s advanced strategies and rapid response capabilities, built on deep industry insight, will be instrumental in accelerating mission outcomes for federal agencies. The combined expertise of CGI and Aeyon will address evolving client needs and create opportunities for employees. The acquisition is backed by Enlightenment Capital, a private investment firm specializing in the Aerospace, Defense, Government & Technology (ADG&T) sector. CGI Federal Inc. is dedicated to partnering with federal agencies to provide solutions for defense, civilian, healthcare, justice, intelligence, and international affairs missions. Founded in 1976, CGI Inc. is one of the largest independent IT and business consulting services firms globally.
