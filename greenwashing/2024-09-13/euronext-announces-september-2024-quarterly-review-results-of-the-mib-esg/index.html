Euronext Announces September 2024 Quarterly Review Results Of The MIB ESG

Euronext has announced the results of the quarterly review for the MIB ESG index, which will be implemented on Monday, September 23, 2024. The review includes the inclusion of MFE-MEDIAFOREUROPE N.V. and the exclusion of BREMBO S.p.A. Euronext reserves the right to modify the published selection until the final data is released after Wednesday, September 18, 2024. Any events occurring after this date will not result in the replacement of a company that may need to be removed from the final selection. The MIB ESG index undergoes quarterly reviews in March, September, and December, with a full annual review in June. The next review is scheduled for Friday, December 13, 2024. In summary, the MIB ESG index will be updated on September 23, 2024, with the inclusion of MFE-MEDIAFOREUROPE N.V. and the exclusion of BREMBO S.p.A. Euronext has the authority to make changes until the final data is published on September 18, 2024. The index is reviewed quarterly and annually, with the next review set for December 13, 2024.
