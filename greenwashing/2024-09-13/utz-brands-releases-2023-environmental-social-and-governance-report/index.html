Utz Brands Releases 2023 Environmental , Social , and Governance Report

Utz Brands, Inc., a prominent U.S. manufacturer of branded Salty Snacks and Staples equity, released its 2023 Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Report. The report, covering the fiscal year ending December 31, 2023, showcases the company s progress in ESG areas, building on the momentum from its 2022 report. Environment: Utz has made strides in reducing carbon emissions through network optimization, efficient distribution strategies, and minimizing food waste contributions to landfills. The company has also developed a comprehensive environmental policy. Social: Utz prioritizes people and fosters an inclusive work environment that focuses on the well-being and development of its associates. The company s workforce diversity initiatives aim to provide equal opportunities and representation across all organizational levels. Governance: Utz maintains a strong corporate governance framework that promotes ethical decision-making, accountability, and transparency. The company continuously works to improve its governance practices to maintain stakeholder trust and confidence. For more information on Utz s ESG programs and the full 2023 ESG report, interested parties can visit the company s Sustainability website. Utz Brands, Inc. manufactures a diverse portfolio of savory snacks through popular brands like Utz®, On The Border® Chips & Dips, Zapp’s®, and Boulder Canyon®. The company, based in Hanover, Pennsylvania, has multiple manufacturing facilities across the U.S. to serve its growing customer base. Investors and others interested in the company are encouraged to review material financial information on Utz s Investor Relations website, as well as through the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) filings, press releases, public conference calls, and webcasts. Utz also uses social media channels to communicate with stockholders and the public about the company, its products, and other relevant information.
