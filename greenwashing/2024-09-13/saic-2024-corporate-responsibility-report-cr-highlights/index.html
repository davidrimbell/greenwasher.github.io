SAIC 2024 Corporate Responsibility Report : CR Highlights

SAIC s 2024 Corporate Responsibility Report highlights the company s commitment to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles, sustainability, and positive community and global impact. The report showcases SAIC s internal focus on sustainability efforts and its external empowerment of customers through science and technology applications to address environmental challenges and contribute to climate resilience and change discussions. Since 2019, SAIC has achieved a significant reduction in Scope 1 and 2 greenhouse gas emissions by 41%. The report emphasizes the company s values, which include delivering excellent service to customers, providing enriching careers for employees, and upholding societal and environmental responsibilities while contributing outstanding value to the business. The report is the fifth annual publication of SAIC s dedication to ESG principles and sustainability. It includes data and activities for FY24, ending February 2, 2024, and follows globally accepted sustainability reporting frameworks, such as the Global Reporting Initiative Standards 2021, the Sustainability Accounting Standards Board, the Task Force on Climate-Related Financial Disclosures, and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. The SAIC ESG Reporting Index, TCFD Report, and SAIC s response to the GRI Index are also included in the report. The company encourages readers to contact its ESG team for any questions or additional information. In summary, SAIC s 2024 Corporate Responsibility Report demonstrates the company s ongoing commitment to sustainability, ESG principles, and positive impact on communities and the environment. The report reflects SAIC s values and achievements, while also acknowledging the need for continued efforts to combat climate change and create a brighter future for the world and our planet.
