Boutique Design Announces 2024 Boutique 18 Class

Boutique Design magazine has announced the 2024 class of the Boutique 18, highlighting the next wave of designers who are transforming the hospitality industry. This year s awardees, selected by their peers and the magazine s editorial team, are recognized for their groundbreaking work in pioneering projects. These talented individuals are redefining creativity and design excellence. The honorees include David Aleman, director of design + project management at Marriott International; Tessa Bates, senior interior designer at Champalimaud; Laura Bernier, design director at IHG; Justin Brown, senior interior design manager at KTGY; Kali Bruhnke, interior designer at ESG Architecture & Design; Liz Dalton, senior interior designer at Arcsine; Amanda Davis, design lead at Meyer Davis; Erik Diaz, senior manager at Hyatt Hotels Corporation; Alexis Gillum-Cousby, associate interior designer at Gensler; Alex Hach, interior designer + senior associate at DLR Group; Louis Iannone, director of interior design at Eimer Design; Kate Li, senior designer at RD Jones & Associates; Marie Perriello, senior interior designer at Flick Mars; Cesar Ramirez, senior designer at KNA Design; Cheyenne Rush, interior designer at VANROOY Design; Melissa Shalloway, senior interior designer at Morris Adjmi Architects; Heather Shapiro, senior project designer at Avenue Interior Design; and Mrinal Suri, senior project director at HBA San Francisco. The 2024 B18 honorees, along with BD s 2024 Up-and-Coming Hotelier of the Year, Neena Gupta, will be featured in the Fall 2024 issue of Boutique Design magazine. The Gold Key Awards gala, where these talented individuals will be celebrated, will take place on November 11th at Cipriani 42nd Street in New York. Tickets are available for purchase now.
