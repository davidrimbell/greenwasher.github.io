ESG Investing : A Global Regulatory Review and Outlook | Morgan Lewis

In 2024, Morgan Lewis has been examining the complex regulatory frameworks that influence ESG (environmental, social, and governance) investments in major global markets such as the United States, the European Union, the United Kingdom, the Asia-Pacific region, the Middle East, and Africa. These regions, each with their distinct economic, environmental, and political landscapes, present unique challenges and opportunities for ESG investing. The evolving regulatory environments in these areas are shaping the global trends in sustainable finance, as ESG considerations become increasingly crucial to investment strategies worldwide. The firm s analysis aims to understand the specificities of each region s approach to ESG regulations, thereby contributing to a more comprehensive understanding of the global ESG investment landscape.
