Swiss minister promotes green finance hub

Switzerland is one of the most environmentally-friendly financial sectors in the world, according to the country's finance minister Ueli Maurer. He says it could be a pioneer for sustainable investment.The Swiss government has said it is too early to legislate on sustainability.
