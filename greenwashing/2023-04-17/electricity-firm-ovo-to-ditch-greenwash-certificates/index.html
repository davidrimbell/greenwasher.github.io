Electricity firm Ovo to ditch greenwash certificates

Energy firm Ovo Energy has announced plans to raise £25m to help customers save money from a lack of green energy guarantees, which it says would be the biggest increase in green electricity tariffs in the UK.The company has said it will use the money saved to offer customers visits from energy-saving advisers.
