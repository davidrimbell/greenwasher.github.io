Japan Proposes New Guidelines on ESG Public Funds in Response to Greenwashing Concerns | Morgan Lewis

Japan has introduced new guidelines for public investment trust funds in order to avoid a "greenwashing" of environmental investments. Environmental, social, and governance are among those created under the financial instruments and exchange laws of the country.
