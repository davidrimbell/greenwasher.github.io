Vanguard ESG International Stock ETF ( BATS : VSGX ) Shares Sold by Merriman Wealth Management LLC

Merriman Wealth Management LLC reduced its stake in Vanguard ESG International Stock ETF (VSGX) by 12.9% during the second quarter, selling 1,776 shares and leaving it with 11,949 shares valued at $684,000. Other institutional investors also adjusted their holdings in VSGX during the same period. Archer Investment Corp acquired a new stake worth $50,000 in the second quarter, while Voisard Asset Management Group Inc. and Fortis Group Advisors LLC made similar investments in the fourth quarter, worth $58,000 and $71,000 respectively. Union Savings Bank also increased its position in VSGX by $124,000 in the second quarter. Coston McIsaac & Partners raised its stake by 9.6% in the first quarter, owning 2,378 shares worth $136,000. VSGX, an ETF based on the FTSE Global All Cap ex USA Choice index, screens global ex-US companies for ESG criteria. Launched in September 2018, it is managed by Vanguard. The ETF s stock price opened at $60.22 on Friday, with a 50-day simple moving average of $58.84 and a 200-day simple moving average of $57.84.
