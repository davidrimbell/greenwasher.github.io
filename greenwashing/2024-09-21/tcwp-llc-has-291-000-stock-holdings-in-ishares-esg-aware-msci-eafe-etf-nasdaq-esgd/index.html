Tcwp LLC Has $291 , 000 Stock Holdings in iShares ESG Aware MSCI EAFE ETF ( NASDAQ : ESGD )

Tcwp LLC increased its stake in iShares ESG Aware MSCI EAFE ETF (NASDAQ:ESGD) by 12.5% during the second quarter, owning 3,693 shares worth $291,000. Other significant investors also adjusted their positions: TIAA Trust National Association increased its holdings by 0.8%, owning 5,008,504 shares valued at $400,230,000; Envestnet Asset Management Inc. raised its stake by 4.8%, owning 1,629,400 shares worth $128,364,000; Goldman Sachs Group Inc. increased its holdings by 0.6%, owning 1,612,149 shares valued at $121,782,000; Spinnaker Trust boosted its holdings by 0.6%, owning 1,189,313 shares worth $93,694,000. Mercer Global Advisors Inc. ADV also raised its holdings by 8.0%, owning 985,020 shares valued at $77,483,000. The iShares ESG Aware MSCI EAFE ETF experienced a 1.1% drop in shares, opening at $82.71 on Friday. The fund has a market cap of $8.52 billion, a PE ratio of 15.45, and a beta of 0.86. The fund is managed by BlackRock and tracks the MSCI EAFE Extended ESG Focus index, focusing on companies with positive environmental, social, and governance characteristics.
