BGE will be the first utility in Maryland to use biogas from anaerobic digesters

A Maryland utility has become the first in the US state to use biogas in its distribution system, according to a report from the World Trade Organization (WTOP). Baltimore Gas and Electric Company (BGE) has been given the green light by the public.
